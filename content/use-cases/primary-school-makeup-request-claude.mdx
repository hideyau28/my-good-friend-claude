## 情境

5 月 3 日 8:14pm。

你細路 Lily (P3, 9 歲) handwriting 把今日中文默書 paper 拎出嚟。Sticky note from teacher:「請家長 sign」。

你 see grade: **13/100**.

你 deep breath. 50 個生字, Lily 識 7 個.

你 ask: 「點解咁?」

Lily: 「嘅我 forgot study. 我 watch YouTube 嗰晚.」

你 ask: 「你媽咪 唔 知 你 要 默書嗎?」

Lily: 「我 唔 update homework book.」

你 ask: 「咁 你 知 你 fail 啦?」

Lily: 「Miss Cheung 話 13 分 lowest in class.」

你 ask: 「如果 比 你 一次 chance, 你 study 嗎?」

Lily: 「Yes! I serious!」

你 weigh 同 partner:

- **Option A**: Sign paper, accept 13 grade, 我哋 manage 大 picture (學業 still mid-range OK, 1 default 唔 catastrophic)
- **Option B**: Apply makeup test (re-take exam)
  - 學校 policy: parent's letter required justify request
  - Approval rate: estimated 30-50% (admit teacher discretion)
  - If approved: Lily prep + retake
  - If denied: same as Option A
- **Option C**: 補習班 加 一個 next 1 個月 prevent recurrence

你 揀 Option B (give chance + parent accountability), 開 phone 寫信 8:34pm. 你 type:

> 「Dear Miss Cheung, our daughter Lily got a low mark on yesterday's dictation. We are very sorry. Can she retake the test? Thank you.」

讀完 cringe — sounds desperate.

你 try:

> 「Dear Miss Cheung, Lily failed her dictation 因為 she forgot. We respectfully request opportunity for her to retake to demonstrate her actual capability.」

讀完更 cringe — sounds defensive.

呢個 makeup test letter paradox:

- **「對唔住」 over-apologize** — teacher think 你 entitled parent expecting favors
- **「Lily forgot」** — teacher see 「parents excusing kid」
- **「Demonstrate her actual capability」** — teacher think 你 dispute test validity
- **太 brief** — teacher dismiss as not serious request
- **太 long** — teacher annoyed reading

你 want: teacher **read 知 你 parents took accountability + Lily took responsibility + specific learning evidence + reasonable ask**.

呢篇用 Claude 20 分鐘 寫到 **specific evidence + reasonable ask + parent accountability** — teacher say yes 機會 30% → 80%.

## 跟住做

### 1. Dump situation honestly + identify what 「good evidence」 looks like

開 Claude 之前 dump 真實 picture:

> **Lily situation**
> - P3, 9 歲, 中文 班 mid-class
> - Default 中文 成績 60-75 range
> - Today's dictation: 13/100
> - 原因: 唔 prep ("forgot")
> - Lily reaction: 沒 cry, sober, asked for chance
>
> **過往 pattern**
> - 中文 一般 OK 但 not 強 subject
> - 默書 typical: 55-75 range
> - Dropped 50+ percentile 唯一 一次
> - 學期 中段 (mid-term): grade still OK overall
>
> **What teacher knows about us**
> - Teacher: Miss Cheung, P3 中文, 8 年 experience
> - 我哋 attended parent-teacher day in Oct (formal)
> - 我哋 reachable by 家長 平台 / phone
> - We have not 拖 contact teacher before
> - Lily not known as「troublemaker」 in class
>
> **我 want**
> - 1 chance makeup test
> - 重要: NOT to「erase」13 grade — but to show real capability
> - Lily takes prep 認真 next week
> - 我哋 parents involved in prep
> - 唔 demand teacher
>
> **我 prep evidence (within 1 week if approved)**
> - Lily own re-study plan
> - 50 word list re-practice
> - Parent monitor / partner with study
> - 1 written reflection from Lily「why I failed + what I'll do differently」 (P3 level)
>
> **What 我 know about 學校 policy**
> - 學校 policy: makeup tests granted at teacher discretion

> - Usually approved if: parent communicates accountability + child shows preparation

> **我 fear**
> - Teacher denies → Lily disappointed

> - 我 letter sets wrong tone → teacher question 我 parenting

### 2. 用呢個 prompt 出 makeup test request letter

<PromptBlock label="完整 prompt — Primary school makeup test request letter">
[paste 你 dump]

請整 **makeup test request letter** to Miss Cheung:

📐 **Structure (1 頁 A4 max)**

**Header**
- Date
- Your name + relation (parent)
- 細路 name + class
- Subject + relevant 試 / test reference

**Opening (2-3 sentence)**
- 唔 over-apologize
- 唔 dramatize
- 簡 statement:「I'm writing to request a makeup opportunity for Lily's dictation test (50 字, score 13/100, dated [日期])」

**Paragraph 1: Take accountability**
- 不 blame Lily alone, 不 blame ourselves alone
- Specific:「Lily did not adequately prepare. We as parents did not check in on her preparation that week.」
- 不 「we are very sorry」 repetitive
- 1 acknowledge:「13/100 is below Lily's usual range (60-75) and reflects missing preparation, not lack of capability.」

**Paragraph 2: 細路 ownership**
- 1 sentence Lily's own reflection (paraphrase her words):
- 「Lily acknowledged she did not study. She has expressed she is ready to prepare seriously if given another chance.」
- 不 「Lily promises to do better」 (vague)
- Specific evidence Lily 已 started reflection (見 prep section)

**Paragraph 3: Specific prep plan**
- 「If approved, our re-preparation plan:」
  - 「Daily 20-min review of the 50 字 word list, Mon-Fri next week」
  - 「Practice 默書 daily with parent quiz」
  - 「Lily own written reflection (attached)」
  - 「Parent sign each day's practice notebook」
- 1 timeframe:「Re-takable within 7 day, schedule at 你 convenience」

**Paragraph 4: 不 demanding tone**
- 「We understand makeup tests are at 你 discretion and are exception, 不 standard practice.」
- 「If approved, we appreciate the additional opportunity. If 不, we accept the original grade.」
- 不 over-pressure teacher

**Closing (1 sentence)**
- 「Thank you for considering. We are reachable at [phone] / [email] if you need to discuss.」
- Sign + date

📋 **Attached evidence (1 page)**

- 「Lily own reflection note」(in Lily's own handwriting):
  - 「I forgot to study because I watched YouTube」
  - 「I am sorry to Miss Cheung」
  - 「My plan: 每日 review 字, ask Mom or Dad to test me, re-take 試」
  - 「I will be honest about my preparation this time」

- Parent prep schedule (signed by 你 or partner):
  - Day 1: 字 list review + 練習
  - Day 2: 字 list review + 練習
  - Day 3: practice test 1 (parent monitor)
  - Day 4: weak words 加 focus
  - Day 5: practice test 2 (parent monitor)
  - Day 6-7: rest
  - Day 8: makeup test

🚫 **Avoid phrases**
- 「Sorry to bother 你」(submissive)
- 「Please understand my child is really not like this」 (excuse)
- 「Other parents...」 (irrelevant)
- 「She'll be heartbroken if 唔 retake」 (emotional manipulation)
- 「I know teaching is busy but...」 (passive aggressive)

✅ **Include phrases**
- 「Accountability」
- 「Capability vs preparation」
- 「Reasonable opportunity」
- 「Thank you for considering」
- 「We accept either decision」

📝 **Tone**

- Adult-to-adult professional
- Equal partner with teacher
- 不 deferential, 不 demanding
- Specific evidence + plan
- Mutually respectful

語氣：collaborative request, prepared family.
</PromptBlock>

Claude 出 draft. **Read 1 次, sit 1 hour, read 第 2 次 fresh**. 點 polish:

- Detail too school-specific 你 adjust
- Voice 太 「professional」 cold — soften 1 phrase
- Lily reflection note 用 her own words — 我 (parent) help structure, 不 ghost-write

### 3. Lily 1 頁 reflection note (P3 level, her own writing)

家長 letter 同時, Lily own reflection 同樣重要:

<PromptBlock label="完整 prompt — P3 student own reflection note structure">
我幫 Lily write own reflection note. Lily age 9, P3 level 中文 written.

📐 **Structure for 9 歲 reflection (P3-level vocabulary)**

**1 段 (5-7 sentence)** in her own handwriting:

Hook:「Miss Cheung,」

**Sentence 1: Honest fact**
- 「我 default book 沒有 check」OR「我 forgot 學習 字」
- 不 excuse, 不 blame YouTube alone

**Sentence 2: Feeling**
- 「我 sad about 13 分.」
- 不 perform emotion, real word

**Sentence 3: Realization**
- 「呢 50 字 我 6 月 仲 useful, 我 知 我 need learn.」
- Acknowledge longer-term

**Sentence 4: Plan**
- 「我 plan study 每日 20 分鐘. 媽咪 test me.」
- Specific, parent visible

**Sentence 5: Ask**
- 「如 可以 重 try makeup test, I will prepare. If 不 can, I understand.」
- Reasonable

**Sentence 6: Close**
- 「Thank you Miss Cheung.」

**End: Sign:「Lily」 + date**

🎯 **Voice tips for 9 歲**

- Lily own handwriting (not parent's)
- Lily own word choice (parent assist structure, not vocabulary)
- 5 typo OK (more authentic 不 perfect)
- 1 sentence simple Chinese OK if Lily 默 simple

📋 **Helps teacher see**
- 細路 真 take responsibility
- Reflection 不 ghost-written by parent
- Capable of acknowledgment + plan
- 對 future learning attitude

🚫 **Avoid for Lily reflection**
- Adult phrasing「I deeply regret」(suspicious 細路 wrote)
- Excuse「I had busy week」 (entitled)
- Over-promise「I will get 100/100」 (unrealistic)
- 對 Miss Cheung kissing up「Miss Cheung is best teacher」 (suspicious)

✅ **OK to include**
- 1 細路 honest acknowledgment「我 like YouTube too much sometimes」
- 1 specific commitment「我 will use 字 cards」
- 1 sentence Mom 同 dad role「Mom will check 我 study」

語氣：authentic 9-year-old voice, honest, structured by parent but not ghost-written.
</PromptBlock>

⏰ **Lily 自己 spend 30 分鐘** write own letter. 你 sit nearby, help spell 1-2 字, 但 don't dictate. Lily letter authenticity = teacher trust signal.

### 4. Submit + follow up

<PromptBlock label="完整 prompt — Submit + post-decision protocol">
Letter + Lily reflection ready. Submission + post-decision protocol:

📬 **Submission**

- 學校 家長 platform message OR direct email to Miss Cheung
- 不 hand 細路 deliver (細路 may forget / lose)
- Subject:「Makeup test request — Lily [class], dated [test date]」
- Attach: parent letter (PDF) + Lily reflection note (photo of handwriting)

📅 **Timing**
- Within 3 working days of test result
- Don't wait > 1 week (looks like afterthought)
- Don't submit late evening (looks like rushed)

⏰ **Expected response**
- Teacher reply 1-3 working days typical
- If 4+ days no response: gentle follow-up message
- Subject:「Following up on makeup request — Lily [class]」
- 不 multiple follow-up if 1 follow-up no response (teacher decision time)

✅ **If approved**
- Reply: 「Thank you. Lily and we are ready. Re-take 試 [proposed time]?」
- Confirm logistics in writing
- Lily prep執行 plan (8 day plan from letter)
- Lily attend retake calm

❌ **If denied**
- Reply: 「Thank you for considering. We respect 你 decision. Lily's next dictation will be 認真 prepared.」
- 不 negotiate 唔 push
- Use as learning moment for Lily: 「We asked respectfully, teacher decided 不. That's allowed. We continue forward.」
- Don't bad-mouth teacher in front of Lily

📊 **Re-take outcome**

- 若 Lily score >60: outcome OK, 13 grade lesson processed
- 若 Lily score 低於 50: more serious 學業 conversation needed (Lily extra support, possible tutor)
- 若 Lily score >80: bonus, 不 set unreal expectation for everyday tests

🎯 **Long-term lesson**

- Lily learnt: preparation matters, parents involved, default 不 catastrophic
- Parents learnt: check-in on Lily prep weekly (not just trust 她)
- Teacher learnt: this family communicates accountably
- All: 1 default test 不 trajectory-changing

📋 **Documentation**

- Save letter + response + retake outcome in 1 folder
- 如 future similar situation: reference 過去 communication
- Lily 升 班 next year: not transferred to new teacher unless major

🚫 **Anti-pattern after decision**

- 公婆 / 親朋 discuss teacher decision adversarially in front of Lily
- 細妹 compare:「你 lucky 唔似 sister」
- Family dinner main topic:「Lily failed test」(traumatize Lily)
- 公開 social media post about it
- Letter become recurring resentment

✅ **Healthy framing for Lily**

- Approved + retake good: 「We took accountability + teacher gave chance. Lesson learned.」
- Approved + retake mediocre: 「You tried. Better than 13. Next dictation pre-emptive prep.」
- Denied: 「13 grade stays. We move forward. Next dictation prove yourself.」
- 任何 outcome: 「You handled this maturely. We're proud you took ownership.」

語氣：mature parents, mature 細路, 不 catastrophize.
</PromptBlock>

## 變化

### 變化 1：高年級 (升中 / DSE prep) makeup test

S.1-S.6 嘅 makeup request 同 P3 不同 dynamics:

<PromptBlock label="變化 1 — Secondary school makeup test request">
我細路 [S.1-S.6] 突發 medical / family / 緊急情況 missed exam. 想 apply makeup.

📐 **Secondary makeup process**

📋 **Letter expansion (1.5 頁)**

**Header section**
- Student name + class + form
- Subject + test type (test / quiz / exam / mock)
- 試 date missed + 原因
- Doctor note attached if medical

**Substantive request**

**Reason category** (be specific):
- Medical: 試 hospital letter / GP note (sealed)
- Family emergency: 1 sentence respect privacy
- 突發 (公共 transport disruption, severe weather): 學校 通常 OK if school-wide
- Mental health: 學校 counsellor referral if 已 involved
- Religious / cultural: prior notice 通常 required

**Documentation required:**
- Medical: GP / specialist letter on letterhead
- Family: 家長 letter detailing
- Mental health: counsellor 通信 (consent)
- Religious: confirm with school chaplaincy / 家長 letter

**Academic standing**
- Student current grade trend in subject
- Why missed exam impact disproportionate
- 試 cycle context (mid-term vs final)

**Make-up timing request**
- Within 試 cycle if possible
- Student prep status
- Reasonable date

**Family commitment**
- 家長 oversight prep
- Student own catchup plan
- Parent-teacher partnership

📞 **Communication channel**
- 公私 校 通 不同 platform (e-Class / SAS-OnlinePlus / school portal / direct email)
- 公校: typically slower response
- 私校: typically faster but formal
- Adviser teacher 1st contact, then escalate

⏰ **Timing**
- 24-48 hr of missed exam ideal
- 「Within 7 days」 generally maximum
- Late submission viewed less favorably

🚨 **Boarding / overseas student adjustment**
- Time zone factor
- Pickup / drop-off coordination
- 學校 nurse / doctor involved if applicable

語氣：mature secondary student + parent partnership.
</PromptBlock>

### 變化 2：特殊 需要 細路 (SEN) makeup / 加 time request

SEN 細路 嘅 accommodation 同 standard makeup 不同:

<PromptBlock label="變化 2 — SEN 細路 academic accommodation request">
我細路 SEN [dyslexia / ADHD / autism / 視 障 / etc.]. Want apply for 加 time / accommodation / alternative assessment.

📋 **SEN-specific request structure**

**Documentation required**
- Psychological / educational assessment report (recent < 3 年)
- Specialist diagnosis (psychologist / 公立 SEN team)
- Past accommodation history
- 細路 own awareness + agreement

**Specific ask (be precise)**
- 加 time (10%, 25%, 50% — 學校 standardize)
- Quiet room
- Spell-check tool
- Reader / scribe
- Computer typing
- Break allowance
- 等

**Justification**
- Diagnosis directly relates to assessment difficulty
- Past evidence accommodation helps
- 不 「unfair advantage」 — equal access argument

**Permanent vs temporary**
- Permanent: SEN diagnosis ongoing
- Temporary: injury / 試 anxiety post-incident

🏥 **HK SEN system**

- 公立 學校: SENCO (Special Educational Needs Coordinator)
- 私立 學校: own SEN policy variation
- DSE specific: HKEAA SEN accommodation application (separate process)
- 公務員 / police kindergarten: own protocol

📞 **Process**
1. Initial request to class teacher
2. SENCO / VP referral
3. Formal documentation review
4. Trial accommodation 1-2 試
5. Formal approval

⏰ **Timeline**
- Initial discussion: 1-2 個禮拜 before exam
- 不 last minute (system 容納時間 needed)
- Recurring (each new academic year reconfirm)

🤝 **Family-school partnership**
- 細路 own buy-in critical
- Parent advocacy 平衡 細路 ownership
- 學校 partnership 不 adversarial

🚨 **DSE-specific (S.6)**
- HKEAA application separate from school
- Doctor's letter requirement strict
- Deadline (typically 1 yr before DSE)
- Appeal process if denied

語氣：respect for institutional process, 不 demanding tone.
</PromptBlock>

### 變化 3：細路 嚴重 missed school (病 / family crisis)

長期 missed school 後 catch-up:

<PromptBlock label="變化 3 — Long absence catch-up letter">
細路 [2-4 週 missed school for serious illness / family crisis / 海外 medical treatment]. 返校 catch-up letter.

📋 **Long-absence return letter**

**Substantive disclosure (judicious)**
- Illness / surgery 詳 detail or 私 (parent choice)
- Family event: privacy respected, 細路 emotional state shared briefly
- 公 vs 私 disclosure 唔 同 sympathetic

**Academic catch-up reality**
- 細路 actual capacity 限制
- 不 「we'll catch up everything quickly」 unrealistic
- 1-2 subject priority (其他 OK 慢 catch)

**Specific support ask**
- Notes / handouts during absence (peer / teacher provide)
- Tutorial session 細路 1-on-1 (paid tutor optional)
- 試 makeup or 比 spread out
- Lower-stakes assessment 短期
- Counsellor support if emotional

**Family circumstance briefing**
- 細路 may behave differently for 4-6 週 post-return
- 老師 awareness without exposing 細路 to peer
- Reasonable expectation set

**Timeline**
- Return date
- Catch-up assessment estimate
- Realistic full re-integration estimate
- Family contact schedule

🎯 **Soft return**
- First week: 細路 attend without academic pressure
- 老師 understand reduced participation 短期
- 1 friend 細路 trust for re-entry support
- 親子 daily check-in

⚕️ **Medical adjustment**
- Doctor's letter for limitations (PE / 加 break / 醫 appointment)
- Medication schedule school-aware
- Emergency protocol updated

🧠 **Mental health post-crisis**
- 學校 counsellor referral
- Family therapy if applicable
- 細路 own coping resource

📅 **Re-evaluate 4-6 週**
- Progress check 老師
- Adjust support if needed
- Family meeting if major

語氣：vulnerable 但 prepared, partnership with school.
</PromptBlock>

## 一個 mindset

Makeup test request 嘅 hidden test 唔係 細路 capability — 係 **家長 同 老師 嘅 maturity 對話**.

老師 see 200+ 細路 / year. 老師 see 平均 50 個 parent letter / month. 老師 嘅 mental shortcut: 「Is this family 有 accountable, organized, partnership-able?」 — 30 秒 within 你 letter, the answer 顯而易見.

呢個 mindset 嘅 reframe: 你 letter 唔係 「argue Lily case」 — 係 **demonstrate family operates with maturity**. 老師 say yes to family that 老師 trust 會 take advantage of 機會 not waste.

Claude 嘅 role 喺 letter writing: 你 parent default 多 emotional / defensive / over-justify. Claude container 提供 distance — 你 dump situation, output specific structure. 你 read draft, 認得 邊 tone is 「good parent」 voice vs 「entitled parent」 voice.

最後 reminder：

- ✅ **Specific 「we own this. Here's our plan.」 > vague 「we are sorry」**. Accountability shows.
- ⚠️ **細路 own reflection note 真實 critical**. Ghost-written 100% transparent to 老師. Lily own messy handwriting 真.
- 🎯 **Outcome (approved / denied) 不 reflect 細路 worth**. Both teach valuable lesson.

明日 8am submit. Lily 8 月 1 個禮拜 prep. Either way 你 family 1 個禮拜後 仲 OK.
