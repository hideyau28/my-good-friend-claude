> 急性失眠 (3 個月以下) Claude 可以幫 tracking. 慢性失眠 (>3 個月) **必須 GP visit** — chronic insomnia 通常 underlying cause (anxiety / depression / sleep apnea / 藥). Self-track 唔取代 medical diagnosis.

## 情境

而家係 1:14am。

你 11:30pm 上床，「今晚要早 sleep」決心強烈。Read 30 頁書，瞓低 set alarm 7am。

11:55pm: 仍 awake。Sigh.

12:15am: brain 跳到「明日 9am meeting need prep」。

12:30am: 你 check phone (just to silence rate). Now 你 awake. Phone scroll 20 分鐘.

12:50am: phone down，再 attempt sleep。Brain 想 「呢樣 thinking — let me think it through quickly」。

1:14am: 你 realize 已 1+ hr post-bedtime, sleep window shrinking. Anxiety about not-sleeping (meta-anxiety) 開始 dominate. 「If 我 唔 sleep 而家，明日 destroyed」 thinking loop.

3:20am: 終於 fall asleep.

7:00am: 鬧鐘. 4 hr 睡眠.

7:30am: Brain fog. Coffee. 不知 source.

呢個 cycle repeat 過去 3 個禮拜。你 try：

- 早 sleep (11pm)：仍 1:30am sleep
- 不 phone in bed (Day 5)：Day 1-3 better, Day 4-7 同樣
- Sleep medicine OTC (Melatonin 3mg)：Day 1-2 work, Day 3-7 同樣
- 「Just 唔 think」：唔 work （thinking-stop 嘅 paradox）

呢個 acute insomnia 嘅 root cause：

- **Suspect culprits 太多** (caffeine timing / alcohol / 屏幕 / 焦慮 / exercise timing / 食 / 室溫 / 鬧鐘 / partner snoring / 壓力 / hormonal)
- **冇有系統嘅 data** — your sleep memory 24 hr 後 distorted
- **「Just sleep」 reasoning 不 work** — anxiety + brain over-aroused = paradoxical insomnia
- **隨機 attempt 唔 isolate variable** — 你改咗 3 樣同時，唔 know 邊樣 work

呢篇用 Claude **14 日 sleep log**，有系統咁排查 4 大 culprit，identify 你 personal trigger pattern。

## 跟住做

### 1. Understand 4 大 culprit + assessment

開始 logging 前，先 mental model 邊 4 樣 most-likely culprit：

<PromptBlock label="完整 prompt — Sleep culprit overview + my assessment">
我 acute insomnia 3 個禮拜. 想有系統咁 diagnose 自己 trigger 之 前 self-log.

請 explain 4 大 culprit + 我 self-assess 邊個 highest risk:

🍵 **Culprit 1: Caffeine**
- 半 衰期 5-6 hr (你 2pm coffee = 8pm 仍 50% in system)
- 影響: delay sleep onset + reduce deep sleep quality
- Self-assess:
  - 我 daily cup count: [_]
  - 最後一 cup timing: [_]
  - Drink: [coffee / 茶 / energy drink / chocolate (yes, caffeine)]
- 高 risk if: >3 cup/日 OR last cup after 12pm
- Quick test: 1 週限 morning only (cut off 11am)

🍷 **Culprit 2: Alcohol**
- Mistake-thinking: 「alcohol 幫 sleep」
- Reality: alcohol speed sleep onset BUT severely degrade sleep quality (REM disruption, midnight wake)
- Self-assess:
  - 每週 drink: [_]
  - Evening drink 频繁度: [_]
- 高 risk if: >2 drinks/週 OR any nightly drink
- Quick test: 1 週 zero alcohol after 6pm

📱 **Culprit 3: 屏幕 light + stimulation**
- Blue light delay melatonin
- Mental stimulation (Instagram / 新聞 / 工作 email) high cortisol
- Self-assess:
  - 上床 30 min phone usage: [_]
  - Type: [scroll IG / reply work / 睇片 / read]
  - Lights off + still phone?
- 高 risk if: 上床 30 min phone (任何 type)
- Quick test: 1 週 phone outside bedroom by 10pm

🧠 **Culprit 4: 焦慮 / Brain over-arousal**
- 「Anxious thinking」 trigger cortisol release
- Brain treats unsolved problem 為 threat → arousal
- Self-assess:
  - 上床後 random thought intrusion: [_]
  - Anticipatory anxiety about not-sleeping: [_]
  - Daytime stress level 1-10: [_]
- 高 risk if: 上床後 持續 active thinking, daytime stress >7
- Quick test: brain dump journal before bed (next prompt)

🌡 **Honorable mention (smaller impact 但 check):**
- 室溫: optimal 16-19°C (HK 夏天 challenge)
- 鬧鐘 light (digital clock 強光 bother)
- Partner snoring
- Exercise timing (intense exercise < 3 hr before sleep degrades sleep)
- Late heavy meal
- Hormonal (女性 月經 cycle)

📊 **My initial diagnosis self-assessment (1-5 scale)**:
- Caffeine risk: __/5
- Alcohol risk: __/5
- 屏幕 risk: __/5
- 焦慮 risk: __/5

請對 我 assessment, suggest 14 日 elimination order (highest-risk first):
- Day 1-7: focus 邊 culprit
- Day 8-14: focus 邊 culprit

語氣：有系統，no judgment.
</PromptBlock>

Claude 出 your specific priority order。如 caffeine + 焦慮 high risk → Day 1-7 caffeine elimination test，Day 8-14 anxiety protocol test。

📋 **Weekly review prompt**

- 邊 night 最好 sleep? 邊 night 最差?
- Common pattern between 好 night?
- Common pattern between 差 night?

</PromptBlock>

⏰ **Tips for sustainable logging**:
- 設 phone alarm 9:55pm 每日 reminder
- Keep template open in notes app
- 朝 fill before checking email (no contamination from work cortisol)
- 14 日 firm — don't quit Day 5 because 「frustrating no progress yet」

### 3. Brain dump pre-sleep ritual (anxiety culprit)

如果 anxiety high risk, 呢個 step 必須:

<PromptBlock label="完整 prompt — Pre-sleep brain dump ritual">
我 anxiety high risk for insomnia. 我 want a 5-minute pre-sleep journal ritual.

🌃 **Setup (do once)**
- 紙本 journal kept bedside (NOT phone — phone 引 stimulation)
- 1 pen 拎住
- 1 specific time: 上床 前 30 分鐘

📝 **Each night 5-minute structure**:

**Min 1: Brain dump (1 min raw)**
- 寫低 任何 喺 mind 嘅 嘢
- Run-on, 唔 censor
- 5 minutes 前 你 thinking 咩? Worry list? Random?

**Min 2: 「Tomorrow's list」 (1 min)**
- 明日 must-do top 3 items
- 預先 write on paper = brain release the「I must remember」 burden
- 「If 我 唔 sleep, I 仲 know what tomorrow's priorities」

**Min 3: 「Worry catalog」 (1 min)**
- For each worry currently in head:
  - Actionable? Next action: ___
  - Not actionable / future event? 「Worry parking lot — revisit Wednesday」
- Externalize the worry — brain understand 「呢個 is captured, I can release until Wednesday」

**Min 4: 1 gratitude (1 min)**
- 唔好行貨「I'm grateful for health」
- Specific: 「Today's [specific moment] felt good because [specific reason]」
- 不是 self-help cliche — 係 neurological reset to parasympathetic

**Min 5: Body scan (1 min)**
- Lay down
- From toes → head, 1 second per body part
- 注意 tension spots, 不 judge
- Release breath at each

💤 **Then sleep**
- 唔 stress about onset speed
- If 上床 20 分鐘 仍 awake: 起身, light reading 10 分鐘 (NOT phone), 再 return bed
- 唔 watch clock - turn it away
- 5am 完全 awake? Get up — better to be tired evening + push next bedtime ealier rather than stay in bed dreading

📋 **Tools that complement ritual**
- 上床 30 min 前: 1 cup chamomile tea (mild)
- 室溫 18°C
- Black-out curtain (light intrusion disrupt REM)
- White noise machine if street loud
- Magnesium glycinate 200-400mg 1 hr 前 bed (consult GP if 已 服藥)

🚨 **DON'T**
- 唔 supplement OTC sleep aid 持續 >7 日 (dependency risk)
- 唔 alcohol 「to help」 (degrades sleep quality severely)
- 唔 lie in bed >20 min trying to sleep — breaks sleep-bed association

語氣：calming, specific, sustainable ritual.
</PromptBlock>

呢個 ritual 嘅 critical insight: **Brain 嘅 「我必須 think through 呢樣 嘢」 屬於 cognitive arousal**。Externalize 上紙 = brain release 該 「process」 命令 = parasympathetic activate = sleep enable.

### 4. Day 14 review + escalation decision

14 日後做有系統嘅 review:

<PromptBlock label="完整 prompt — Day 14 sleep review + escalation">
我 completed 14 日 sleep log. 請有系統咁 review.

📊 **Paste data summary:**
[14 日 each day key bullets:
- 上床 / sleep onset / wake / duration
- Sleep quality 1-10
- Caffeine timing
- Alcohol Y/N
- 屏幕 ≥ 9:30pm Y/N
- Anxiety daytime 1-10
- Notable]

📈 **Analysis:**

**1. Sleep duration trend**
- 14 日 average
- Trend (improving / stagnant / declining)?
- Variance (consistent or wild)?

**2. Culprit correlation**
- 邊 night 最好 = 共同 pattern?
- 邊 night 最差 = 共同 pattern?
- Specific factor 顯著 correlated with bad sleep?
- 唔 always intuitive (e.g., exercise day 之 night ironically harder sleep)

**3. Compliance to elimination protocol**
- 我 elimination test (caffeine / alcohol / phone) execute 完整?
- Slips? Notable slip 對應 sleep impact?

**4. Intervention effectiveness**
- 邊 intervention worked?
- 邊 intervention 顯無效?
- 邊 we 未 test?

📋 **Verdict**

**Option A: Identified clear trigger + 有 improvement**
- 持續 ongoing protocol
- Re-test 7 日 confirm
- 6 個月後 evaluate sustainability

**Option B: 有 noticeable improvement 但 still 5-6/10 quality**
- Add 1 new variable test next 7 日
- Specific: 邊一個 risk variable 未 isolated?
- 持續 log

**Option C: No clear improvement / persists**
- 重 escalation: GP visit
- Possible underlying issue (anxiety disorder / depression / sleep apnea / 慢性 pain)
- Don't keep self-protocol if 3 個月 cumulative no progress

🚨 **Escalation threshold (Stop self-track + GP):**
- Persistent insomnia >3 個月
- Daytime functioning impaired (work / mood / accident)
- Sleep 少於 5 hr regularly
- Mid-night wake regularly waking 3-4am unable return
- Suspected sleep apnea (loud snoring + 朝 wake exhausted despite 7 hr)
- Self-medicating alcohol / OTC sleep aid 持續

📞 **GP visit prep**
- Bring sleep log (14 日 data)
- Specific complaint:「過去 3 個月持續 insomnia 影響 daily function. Self-track elimination 試 4 culprit, no clear isolatable trigger. 想 explore underlying cause.」
- GP 可能 refer to:
  - Sleep clinic for polysomnography
  - Psychiatrist for anxiety / depression evaluation
  - 慢性 pain / 內科 (if specific complaint)

語氣：data-driven verdict, escalate when appropriate.
</PromptBlock>

## 變化

### 變化 1：Shift work / 通宵 schedule insomnia

夜班 / 三班制 schedule disruption:

<PromptBlock label="變化 1 — Shift work insomnia adaptation">
我 [hospitality / 護士 / 24-hr operations / shift work] 持續 dropdown sleep schedule.

📐 **Shift work circadian challenge**
- Body 天然 evolved for day-active
- Force night active = circadian misalignment
- 慢性 shift workers have higher cardiovascular / mental health risk
- 但 reality: 不 all jobs allow standard hours

✅ **Strategies (within constraints)**

🛏 **Day-sleep optimization**
- 全 blackout curtain（必要，唔係 optional）
- White noise mask daytime noise
- 室溫 cool aggressive (16-17°C)
- 鎖 phone outside room
- Family / co-habitant aware「don't disturb until 4pm」

🍵 **Caffeine strategy**
- Start of shift: full mug
- Mid-shift: small additional
- 4 hr before end-shift: cut off (so 你 sleep 上 bed)

🌞 **Sunlight timing**
- End shift → home: wear sunglasses (block morning light, preserve melatonin)
- Home → bed: dim lights 30 min ramp
- Wake up: 5 minutes outdoor sun (循環 reset)

📅 **Off-day strategy**
- Don't completely flip schedule
- 「Anchor sleep」 中 4-5 hr 一致 across all days
- E.g., always sleep 2pm-9pm minimum, even off-days

🍽 **Meal timing**
- Heavy meal at 「dinner」 (mid-shift for night workers)
- 細 snack mid-shift
- Don't eat 2 hr before sleep regardless of clock time

💪 **Health impact monitoring**
- 每 6 個月 GP check
- Cardiovascular markers
- Mental health check
- Vitamin D (sun deprivation common)

🔚 **Long-term consideration**
- Career trajectory: shift work years sustainable?
- Health cost 真 — 不 ignore
- Negotiate flexibility if option

語氣：realistic, not all problems solvable while shift work persists.
</PromptBlock>

### 變化 2：Sleep apnea suspicion

如果 snoring + daytime exhaustion despite 7+ hr：

<PromptBlock label="變化 2 — Sleep apnea suspicion + medical pathway">
我 noticed:
- 持續 loud snoring (partner / family report)
- 朝 wake exhausted despite 7-8 hr 'sleep'
- Mid-night gasping / choking sensation
- 朝 头痛
- Daytime 困 frequent

請 explain + medical pathway:

🫁 **What sleep apnea is**
- Repeated airway obstruction during sleep
- Body briefly wakes to restart breathing (you don't remember)
- Severe case: hundreds of micro-wake per night
- Sleep quantity OK but quality 嚴重 degraded

⚠️ **Risk factors**
- 男性, 40+
- Overweight / obese (但 also thin people)
- Thick neck circumference
- Family history
- Smoking
- Alcohol (worsen)
- 鼻 sinus / 過敏 obstruction

🏥 **Why critical to diagnose**
- Untreated: cardiovascular disease 2-3x risk
- Stroke risk elevated
- Cognitive impairment
- Workplace accident / car accident risk (drowsy driving)
- Quality of life major impact

📋 **Self-screen (before GP)**
1. Print STOP-BANG questionnaire (free online)
2. Sleep partner observation: count breath pauses / gasping
3. Video / audio record yourself sleeping (phone recording on bedside)
4. Note daytime symptoms

🩺 **GP visit script**
「我 suspect sleep apnea. Past month: persistent loud snoring (witnessed), gasping wake, 朝 头痛, daytime exhaustion despite 8 hr sleep. STOP-BANG score: [X]. 想 refer to sleep clinic for polysomnography.」

🏥 **HK sleep clinic options**
- Public: GP refer to QMH / Princess Margaret Sleep Centre (waitlist 6-12 個月)
- Private: 養和 / 港島東 醫院 Sleep Centre (faster but $$)
- Home sleep test (HSAT): 性 cost-effective initial screen

🛌 **Possible treatment if diagnosed**
- CPAP machine (gold standard, life-changing for OSA)
- Mandibular advancement device (mild case)
- Weight loss (if applicable)
- Positional therapy
- Surgery (rare, severe case)

💰 **Insurance coverage**
- Most HK insurance: diagnostic test + CPAP partially covered
- Pre-authorization 通常 needed
- Sleep clinic 替你 navigate

🚨 **Don't ignore**
- 「Just snoring」 mentality dangerous
- Apnea ≠ 心 weakness — physiology issue
- Treatment dramatically improves quality of life
- Untreated cardiovascular consequence cumulative

語氣：urgent without alarmist.
</PromptBlock>

### 變化 3：Travel / jet lag protocol

跨時區 trip 之 後 insomnia:

<PromptBlock label="變化 3 — Jet lag recovery + travel sleep">
我 trip back from [destination X 時區 diff]. Jet lag affecting sleep [N] 日.

請整 recovery protocol:

✈️ **Jet lag physiology**
- Body 「internal clock」 (circadian) misaligned with new timezone
- Rule of thumb: 1 day recovery per 1 hr time diff
- Eastward (e.g., HK→US East) harder than westward (US→HK)
- Crossover melatonin, cortisol, body temperature cycle

📅 **Pre-trip prep (if anticipated trip)**
- 3-4 days before: shift bedtime 1 hr daily toward destination
- 「Pre-adapt」 minimize lag impact
- Hydrate aggressive

🛫 **In-flight**
- Stay hydrated (3L water for long-haul)
- 限 alcohol + caffeine
- Move every 90 min (circulation + DVT prevention)
- Sleep when destination 夜
- Awake when destination 日

🌅 **Day 1-3 arrival adjustment**
- 早 morning sunlight 30 分鐘 (synchronization signal)
- Force-stay awake until destination bedtime (don't nap > 30 min)
- 早 breakfast / morning routine destination time
- Exercise outdoors (combined sun + movement)
- Melatonin 0.3-1mg 1 hr before destination bedtime (NOT 5mg pharmacy default)
- Avoid alcohol Day 1-3

📋 **Day 4-7 consolidate**
- Sleep should normalize
- Persistent issue past Day 7: usual sleep hygiene baseline check
- 持續 >2 週 = consult GP

💊 **Aids (use sparingly)**
- Melatonin (timing > dose)
- 偶 zolpidem (prescription only, NOT habitual)
- Magnesium for relaxation
- Camomile / valerian tea

🚫 **Don'ts**
- Alcohol as 「sleep aid」 (worse jet lag)
- 「Catch up sleep」 weekend after — push 唔 reset cycle
- Caffeine after noon Day 1-5
- Heavy meal 上 sleep

📊 **Track recovery**
- Daily sleep onset, wake, duration
- Subjective energy
- 期望: full recovery by Day [number of hours timezone diff]

語氣：patient, jet lag is real not weakness.
</PromptBlock>

## 一個 mindset

失眠 嘅 paradoxical truth: **「我 必 sleep」 心 念越 強，越難 sleep**。

呢個 paradox 嘅 mechanism: Brain treat 「failure to sleep」 as a threat. Threat = sympathetic activation = cortisol release = arousal. Arousal antithetical to sleep.

呢個 mindset 嘅 reframe: 14 日 sleep log 嘅 deeper purpose 唔係 「optimize sleep number」——係 **release 你 over-control of sleep**。
- Sleep 唔係 something 你 「achieve」, 係 something 你 「allow」.
- 你 brain over-attentive to sleep impossible to sleep.
- Externalize tracking to Claude / log = brain release control命令.

Claude 嘅 role here: 你 alone tracking 14 日 mental load 高, easy to abandon. Claude container = sustainable, structured, judgment-free. 你 dump data, Claude reflect pattern.

最後 reminder：

- ✅ **3 個月 是 critical timeline**. 3 個月以下 = acute, self-track 可能 sufficient. 3 個月+ = chronic, GP visit mandatory.
- ⚠️ **Don't「make up」 lost sleep**. Sleep 唔係 bank account. Sleep cycle reset 比 sleep duration accumulation 重要.
- 🆘 **如果 你 right now lying awake 2am reading this**: get out of bed, dim light, read for 15 min, return when 真 tired. 不 force sleep in bed.

Sleep well. Or 不 — 都 OK. 14 日後睇 pattern.
