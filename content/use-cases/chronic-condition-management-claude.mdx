> ⚠️ Claude 唔取代 doctor. 文中所有 framework 係 self-tracking 工具，目的係 prep 你下次 GP visit 帶有用 data. 任何 medication / dose / treatment 決定 = doctor only.

## 情境

你 GP visit 3 個月 1 次。每次 15 分鐘。

GP: 「過去 3 個月血糖點?」
你: 「⋯⋯ OK 啦. 上禮拜 fasting 6.2.」
GP: 「Average 點?」
你: 「⋯⋯ 唔 record 嘅. Forgot. Lately seems OK.」
GP: 「Symptoms 點?」
你: 「⋯⋯ 啱啱 4 月嗰陣 sometimes 累. 5 月 OK.」
GP nods, 重 prescribe same medication, schedule next 3-month visit.

呢個 chronic condition 管理 嘅 invisible loss:
- **90 日 你嘅 brain 記住 7 個 data point** (上週 + 重要 spike)
- **180 日 你 forgot 80% trend**
- **GP visit base on 你 verbal report = 10% of actual signal**
- **Medication / dose adjustment less precise than possible**

你 medication 而家 maybe 啱，但 maybe 唔啱。3 個月後你 fasting 突然 7.5 → GP escalate dose 50%。If 你 had data showing actual gradual rise + 2 specific trigger event → GP might tweak 25% instead，or adjust eating schedule.

呢篇用 Claude **monthly chronic condition log**——specifically 設計：

1. 30 分鐘 / month maintenance (sustainable)
2. GP-readable summary at visit time
3. Surface trend / trigger / pattern (你 missed)
4. Empower 你 same as 你 GP empowerment

覆蓋 3 大常見 HK condition：糖尿 (Type 2) / 高血壓 / 哮喘. 框架 universal — adapt for thyroid / IBS / 經痛 / chronic 痛 / autoimmune 都用得.

## 跟住做

### 1. Dump 你 baseline + 整 condition profile

第一次 setup 30 分鐘。一次過 dump 你 medical baseline:

<PromptBlock label="完整 prompt — Chronic condition profile setup">
我 want set up monthly tracking for chronic condition.

📋 **Condition profile**

🏥 **Diagnosis**
- Condition: [Type 2 diabetes / 高血壓 / 哮喘 / 其他]
- Diagnosed when: [date]
- Currently managed by: [GP / specialist]
- Last clinical visit: [date]

💊 **Current medication regimen**
- [Med name 1] [dose] [frequency] [taken since]
- [Med name 2] [dose] [frequency] [taken since]
- 副作用 ever?: [list]

📊 **Most recent labs / measurements**
- HbA1c (糖尿): [value, date]
- Fasting glucose: [value, date]
- 血壓 (3 readings if 高血壓): [values, dates]
- Lung function (哮喘 peak flow): [value, date]
- 其他: [cholesterol, kidney function, etc.]

🎯 **Treatment goal (per doctor)**
- HbA1c target: [少於 7? 少於 6.5?]
- 血壓 target: [少於 130/80? 少於 140/90?]
- Peak flow target: [>80% personal best]

🚨 **Specific 「red flag」 doctor 提醒**
- 我 should immediate call doctor if: [list]
- 我 should immediate ER if: [list]

🍽 **Diet relevant info**
- Major dietary restriction: [low carb? low sodium? gluten-free?]
- Trigger food (哮喘 / IBS 等): [list]
- 我 current pattern: [breakfast / lunch / dinner timing]

🏃 **Exercise current**
- Type + frequency: [walking 3x / week / 等]
- 任何 exercise restriction by doctor?: [yes/no]

😴 **Sleep pattern baseline**
- Average sleep duration: [hr]
- Sleep quality 1-10: [_]

🧠 **Other relevant context**
- Other chronic condition: [list]
- 心理 health (anxiety / depression diagnosis): [yes/no]
- Workplace stress level baseline: [_]

請 review my profile 之後 設計:
1. 月度 tracking 變量 (邊樣 metric most important to log for my specific condition?)
2. 监 frequency for each (daily / weekly / monthly)
3. Specific time-of-day to measure (e.g., 血糖 fasting 朝早)
4. Trigger pattern to specifically look for in my condition

語氣：clinical-aware, respect specialist plan.
</PromptBlock>

Claude 出 personalized monitoring plan。

### 2. Daily quick log (1 minute / day)

Daily log 唔 mental load 重 — quick fill。Form 一致 = 比較 across days:

<PromptBlock label="完整 prompt — Daily chronic condition log template">
請設計 daily 1-minute log template. 我 condition: [X]

📅 **Daily log (filled before bed or evening)**

```
DATE: __________
DAY OF WEEK: __

🩸 **Key metric (specific to condition)**

For 糖尿 (Type 2):
- Fasting glucose 朝: ___ mmol/L
- Post-meal (2 hr after lunch): ___ mmol/L
- 任何 other reading: ___

For 高血壓:
- Morning reading: ___/___
- Evening reading: ___/___
- 任何 anomalous spike: ___

For 哮喘:
- Peak flow 朝: ___ L/min
- 任何 rescue inhaler use today: count ___
- 任何 wheezing / shortness: yes/no, severity 1-5

💊 **Medication compliance**
- 朝 dose taken on time: Y/N
- 晚 dose taken on time: Y/N
- Missed dose: Y/N, which one

🍽 **Diet notable**
- Major deviation from normal diet: [yes/no, what]
- Late meal (after 8pm): Y/N
- Trigger food consumed: [list any]
- Hydration adequate (8 cups water): Y/N

🏃 **Exercise**
- 任何 today: [type + duration]
- Effort 1-10: ___

😴 **Sleep (yesterday)**
- Hours: ___
- Quality 1-10: ___

🧠 **Stress / mood**
- Stress level today 1-10: ___
- Notable event affecting mood: ___

🤒 **Symptoms / sensation**
- Any unusual: [list]
- Energy level 1-10: ___

⚠️ **Red flag check**
- 任何 reading exceeded threshold today?: Y/N
- If Y: what + did 你 contact doctor?
```

⏰ **Filling tips**
- 1 min max — keep template open in Notes app
- 朝 fill what you measure 朝 (glucose / BP / peak flow)
- 晚 fill rest before bed
- Don't「remember backwards」 — log same day or 唔 log
- If miss a day: log skip date, don't fabricate

🎯 **Adherence over perfection**
- 20/30 日 log > 25/30 日 fabricated 5 days
- Honest blank = useful data signal too

語氣：simple, sustainable.
</PromptBlock>

⏰ **Setup time:** Day 1: prep log template + first measurement. Daily 1 min thereafter.

### 3. Monthly review + trend identification

每月 1 次 30 分鐘 review:

<PromptBlock label="完整 prompt — Monthly chronic condition review">
我 completed [N] 月 daily log. 請 monthly review.

📊 **Paste 月度 data summary:**
[Each day's key metric values, medication compliance Y/N, notable events]

📈 **Analyses needed:**

**1. Metric trend (primary metric)**
- Monthly average vs target
- 1st week vs 4th week trend
- Variance (consistent or spiky?)
- Highest / lowest reading + what circumstance
- Comparison to previous month (if available)

**2. Medication adherence**
- 朝 dose miss rate
- 晚 dose miss rate
- Pattern (e.g., always miss when traveling)?
- Adjustment recommend?

**3. Trigger pattern identification**

For 糖尿:
- Post-meal spike correlation with: [specific food / timing / exercise]
- Stress level vs blood sugar correlation
- Sleep quality vs fasting glucose correlation

For 高血壓:
- Time of day pattern (朝 high / 晚 high)
- Day of week pattern (work day vs weekend)
- Sodium intake vs reading
- Sleep quality vs reading
- Caffeine timing

For 哮喘:
- Symptom trigger common (allergen / cold / exercise)
- Rescue inhaler use trend
- Peak flow trend
- Environmental factor (humidity / 工作環境)

**4. Lifestyle factor correlation**
- Exercise day vs metric: better / worse / same?
- Stress 高 day vs metric correlation
- Sleep quality vs metric correlation

**5. Concerning trend (require GP attention)**
- 任何 metric persistently breach threshold?
- 任何 medication side effect emerging?
- 任何 new symptom?

📋 **Output: Monthly summary for GP visit**

Format my data as 1-page summary I can show GP:
- Top-line metric trend
- 主要 trigger pattern identified
- Specific question for GP (3-5 question, ranked)
- Medication compliance honest report
- 任何 concern want explicitly discussed

🚨 **Escalation check**
基於 month 數據, 我應 schedule unscheduled visit?
- 任何 reading >threshold 連續 3+ 日: yes
- New symptom not previously discussed: yes
- 任何 medication 副作用 concerning: yes
- Otherwise: scheduled visit OK

語氣：data-driven, actionable, respect clinical autonomy.
</PromptBlock>

每月 review 嘅 hidden value: **identify trigger 你 daily 太 immersed to see**。第 1 個月可能 noise，第 2-3 個月 pattern emerge.

### 4. Quarterly GP visit prep

3 個月 visit 前 1 個禮拜，integrate 3 個月 monthly review:

<PromptBlock label="完整 prompt — Quarterly GP visit prep">
我 next GP visit 在 [date]. 過去 3 個月 monthly log. 請整 visit prep.

📋 **Print 1-page handout (give to GP)**

**Top section:**
- 我嘅 condition + medication regimen
- Time since diagnosis

**Metric chart (last 90 days):**
- 主 metric trend (paste data)
- Average / range / variance
- Comparison to last quarter
- vs. clinical target

**Pattern identified:**
- Top 3 trigger pattern 我哋 noticed
- Lifestyle factor correlation
- Concerning trend (if any)

**Specific question for GP (3-5):**
- 「[Specific symptom] 持續 [Y] 週, related to [pattern]. Adjust treatment?」
- 「Medication X 副作用 [specific], 揀其他 option?」
- 「Lab work 應該 frequency?」
- 「[Lifestyle change A] feasible for [improvement Z]?」

**Update on doctor's previous recommendation:**
- Recommendation: [X]
- 我 compliance: [%]
- Outcome: [results]

⏰ **Visit logistics**
- 15 分鐘 visit, present 1-page summary first 2 分鐘
- Save 5 分鐘 for question
- Don't try to explain everything — let GP review summary

🎯 **What to bring**
- 1-page summary print
- Current medication bottles (or list)
- Insurance card
- Past lab reports (last 3 visits)
- Any new symptoms documented

💊 **Medication discussion prep**
- 任何 side effect to flag
- 任何 reason want to reduce / stop / switch
- Refill schedule check

📅 **Schedule next visit before leaving**
- Don't「I'll call later」 — book before exit
- 早 booking ensures next 3-month continuity

🚨 **Don't**
- Hide non-compliance (GP can adjust if knows)
- Self-stop medication without telling
- Self-diagnose new condition
- Compare to friend / family treatment

語氣：prepared partnership, not deference.
</PromptBlock>

呢個 prep 嘅 critical insight: GP visits 唔係 你 「report 完問題」, 係 collaborative session. 你 prep with data = GP 有 real signal to work with = treatment 更精準.

## 變化

### 變化 1：Multiple condition (共病) management

如 你 同時 manage 2-3 condition:

<PromptBlock label="變化 1 — Multiple condition management">
我同時 manage [糖尿 + 高血壓 + 高血脂 / 等] [N] condition. 請整 integrated tracking.

🎯 **Multi-condition 挑戰**
- 互動 effect (e.g., 糖尿 加 高血壓 increase 心血管 risk)
- Medication interaction
- Different specialist / different schedule
- Lifestyle factor overlap (diet affects 多 condition)
- Cognitive load 高 (track 5+ daily metric)

📊 **Integrated log design**

```
DAILY (1.5 min):
- Glucose fasting 朝
- BP 朝 + 晚
- Peak flow (if 哮喘 too)
- Cholesterol diet adherence Y/N
- Medication 朝 + 晚 Y/N (all medications)
- Notable symptom from any condition
```

📅 **Combined visit consideration**
- 1 GP coordinate 所有 condition (preferred)
- Or specialist-level coordination
- Bring全部 data 1 visit

💊 **Medication interaction check**
- Pharmacist annual review (free service)
- Bring全部 medication 1 bag
- Check OTC + supplement interaction

🍽 **Diet integration**
- 1 diet plan addressing multiple condition
- Mediterranean / DASH compatible for 糖尿 + 心 health + 高血壓
- 唔 try 3 different diet 同時

🏃 **Exercise integration**
- 中度 intensity sustainable for all condition
- Walking + light resistance 通常 universally safe
- High-intensity restricted (specific condition)

🧠 **Mental health overlap**
- 慢性 condition + depression risk elevated
- Track mental health metric alongside
- Tap therapist / counselor 1 of network

🚨 **Red flag integration**
- 1 condition symptom may signal 其他 condition
- E.g., 糖尿 + sudden BP spike = cardiovascular review

語氣：comprehensive but not overwhelming.
</PromptBlock>

### 變化 2：女性月經 / hormone-related tracking

女性 hormonal cycle 影響 chronic condition:

<PromptBlock label="變化 2 — 女性 cycle-aware chronic tracking">
我 female, [疾病 X] + 月經 cycle. 請 cycle-sync tracking.

📐 **Cycle phase impact on common condition**

🩸 **Menstrual phase (Day 1-7)**
- Pain / cramping disrupting other condition管理
- Mood fluctuation
- 血糖 fluctuation (insulin sensitivity changes)
- 哮喘 worsening (estrogen drop)

🌱 **Follicular phase (Day 7-14)**
- Energy high
- 血糖 control easier
- 哮喘 generally stable

🥚 **Ovulation (Day 14-16)**
- Slight body temperature rise
- 血糖 control stable
- Some 哮喘 mid-cycle worsening

🌖 **Luteal phase (Day 16-28)**
- Insulin resistance increase
- 血糖 trend up
- PMS overlay (mood, energy, appetite)
- 哮喘 pre-menstrual worsening

📊 **Integrated tracking**

```
Beyond standard log, add daily:
- Cycle day
- Estimated phase
- Mood / energy
- Cycle-related symptom
```

📈 **Monthly + cycle review**
- Metric vs cycle phase pattern
- Specific phase 對 condition treatment 唔同?
- Premenstrual flare-up consistent?

⚕️ **Discussion with GP**
- If clear cycle correlation: discuss cycle-aware adjustment
- E.g., 糖尿 luteal phase insulin slight dose change
- E.g., 哮喘 premenstrual prophylactic inhaler

🚨 **Cycle red flag**
- 不 regular cycle (3+ 個月 absent): GP visit (PCOS / thyroid possible)
- 嚴重 dysmenorrhea (period pain disabling): GP
- Mid-cycle bleeding: GP

語氣：respect cycle as data dimension.
</PromptBlock>

### 變化 3：Pediatric / 細路 condition (parent-managed)

家長 manage 細路 condition (兒童哮喘 / type 1 糖尿 / 食物 allergy):

<PromptBlock label="變化 3 — Parent-managed pediatric condition">
我 manage [小朋友] [N 歲]'s [condition]. 我 想 track + GP visit prep.

🧒 **Pediatric tracking specifics**

📋 **Parent log (you fill, not 小朋友)**

```
DAILY (2-3 min):
- Symptom: [list]
- Medication: 朝 + 晚 + as needed Y/N
- Trigger encountered today: [list]
- Sleep quality 1-10
- 食 schedule
- Exercise / activity level
- School / day-care report (any incident)
- 任何 unusual mood / behavior
```

🏥 **Specific to common pediatric**

**Asthma (兒童 most common chronic)**
- Trigger track: pet / dust / cold / exercise / cold weather / 病
- Peak flow if 6+ 歲 + cooperative
- Rescue inhaler use count
- ER visit / hospitalization Y/N

**Eczema (atopic dermatitis)**
- Flare location + severity
- Trigger food / environment
- Moisturizer compliance Y/N
- Itch score 1-10

**Food allergy**
- Cross-contamination event
- Hidden ingredient encounter
- EpiPen check (expired?)
- School / 親朋 awareness?

👨‍⚕️ **Pediatrician visit prep**
- 1-page summary
- Growth tracking (height / weight / vs growth curve)
- 學校 / 課外 impact
- 教養 / 家庭 dynamic impact
- 細路 own report (if old enough)

🎓 **School coordination**
- 學校 nurse aware
- Action plan filed
- Medication left at school?
- 老師 inform

🧠 **Emotional support 細路**
- 細路 understand condition (age-appropriate)
- Manage anxiety / shame around being「different」
- 同朋輩 disclosure strategy

🚨 **Red flag**
- Treatment plan not working (>2 月 no improvement) — 2nd opinion
- 細路 mental health concern: psychologist referral
- Family burnout from caregiving — respite + support group

語氣：empower parent, child still own agency where possible.
</PromptBlock>

## 一個 mindset

慢性 condition 管理 嘅 paradox：你係 condition 嘅 daily expert，但 GP 係 medical decision authority。

呢個 paradox 嘅 source: GP visit 15 分鐘 / 3 個月 = 0.0017% of your 「condition lived experience」 time. 你 90 日 × 24 hr = 2160 hr of lived experience，GP gets 15 min。

呢個 mindset 嘅 reframe: 你 monthly log 唔係 「self-treatment」 — 係 **expand the 0.0017% signal availability to GP**. GP 而家 work with 2-page data 而非 30-second verbal report. Treatment precision dramatically increase.

Claude 嘅 role 喺 chronic management: 你 alone tracking sustainable < 6 個月 (cognitive load 太重). Claude container + structured analysis = 6 個月 → 5 年 sustainable. 你 dump data daily, Claude reflect pattern monthly, you brief GP quarterly.

最後 reminder：

- ✅ **Honest non-compliance > fabricated compliance**. GP adjust based on what 你 actually do, 唔係 你 「應該」 do.
- ⚠️ **Medication side effect > 你 think是 「自然 老 process」**. 任何 new symptom emerging post-medication start = flag GP.
- 🆘 **Threshold-breaking reading** (例 血糖 >15 mmol/L random, BP >180/110, peak flow 低於 50% personal best): **不 wait next scheduled visit. Call now / urgent care**.

90 日後 你 GP visit 帶住 data ride show up. GP 第 1 次見你 prep 充分嘅 patient — 對 treatment trajectory 改善 long-term significant.
