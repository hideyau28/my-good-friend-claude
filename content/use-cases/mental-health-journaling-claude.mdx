## 情境

11:23pm。你瞓喺床，眼望天花板。

腦袋自動 replay 今日：

- 早 10:30 老闆 一句「呢個 deck 嘅 framing 你 reconsider 下」——你成日問自己佢真正意思
- 中午 lunch 同同事傾到一半，你講錯某句嘢，佢面色變咗 0.5 秒——你而家確認佢覺得你 unprofessional
- 4pm 你發現 forgot 一個 deadline，pushed 1 日，但你 brain insist 「老闆會記得 + 影響你 year-end review」
- 6pm 同 partner phone call，佢 「whatever」 嘅 tone 你判斷係 passive aggressive
- 8pm 你 scroll IG 見同學買樓，你 net worth 突然清晰咁映入腦

12 個小時嘅 conversation + interaction + observation，全部 dumped into 同一個未過濾嘅 mental tab。

呢類 night-time mental loop 唔係性格弱——係 brain 嘅 default state when input >> processing capacity。Without an externalize ritual, 你 brain 用 4 hours 處理嗮 5 分鐘 dialogue 嘅 nuance。

呢篇用 Claude 每晚 **10 分鐘 structured journal**——唔係取代 therapy，係：

1. 幫你 brain dump（externalize）
2. Identify pattern across days
3. 區分 「realistic concern」 vs 「rumination loop」
4. Sleep 更易（externalize 之後 brain 少 process）

文末有 **HK 真人 mental health support helpline + therapist referral**。Claude 唔取代 professional。

## 跟住做

### 1. Understand 個 boundary first

開 Claude 之前，先 internalize 呢個 boundary：

**✅ Claude 幫到嘅嘢**
- Brain dump container（無 judgment）
- Structure repetitive thought 變 reviewable text
- Identify pattern across weeks
- Suggest reframing techniques（CBT-light）
- 24/7 available
- 唔需要約 appointment / pay

**❌ Claude 唔可以做嘅嘢**
- Diagnose depression / anxiety / PTSD
- 處理 acute crisis（self-harm thoughts / suicidal ideation）
- 取代 long-term therapist relationship
- Prescribe 藥
- 提供 emergency intervention

**⚠️ Red flag — 即係 stop Claude + 揾真人**
- 持續 >2 週 嘅 daily sadness
- Daily functioning impaired
- Sleep / appetite disturbed >2 週
- Self-harm thoughts
- Isolation from all social contact

如果你而家 in any of these signs，**now is the time to call**，唔係 read 呢篇 article。HK helpline 在文末。

### 2. 用呢個 prompt 起 daily journal session

每晚 same time（建議 10-11pm，sleep 前）開 Claude：

<PromptBlock label="完整 prompt — Daily mental health journal session">
我想用你做 daily journal partner。我會 dump 今日嘅 mental state，請幫我 structure 同 reflect。

📝 **今日 brain dump (raw, unfiltered)**

[寫 5-10 分鐘 stream-of-consciousness — 今日嘅對話、emotion、worry、physical sensation、anything 喺 你 mind. 唔需要 polished sentence. Run-on 都 OK]

⏰ **今日嘅 baseline**
- Sleep last night: [hours]
- 主要 stressors today: [bullet points]
- 主要 positive moments today: [bullet points if any]
- 我嘅 physical state right now: [tired / wired / numb / 過度 stimulated]
- 我 mood 1-10: [number]

---

請幫我做 3 樣嘢:

**1. Reflect back 3 個主要 themes**
- 唔評論，只係 mirror back
- 「你今日寫嘅嘢，3 個 themes 浮現：[a, b, c]」
- Themes 可以係 emotion / pattern / question

**2. Distinguish 「realistic concern」 vs 「rumination」**
- 對於我 dumped 嘅嘢，哪些係 actionable concern? Action 係咩?
- 哪些係 brain looping 無 actionable answer? 點 catch the loop?
- 唔好 dismissive — 「呢樣只係 rumination」 嘅 framing 反而傷
- Frame as：「呢個 thought 你已經 process 5 次相同 detail，brain 唔會 此夜 reach new insight」

**3. 一個 small reframe（CBT-light）**
- 揀 1 個 distortion 我 wrote about（e.g., catastrophizing, mind-reading, all-or-nothing）
- 短小 reframe 提示，唔 lecture
- 「你寫『老闆覺得我 unprofessional』 — 可能 alternative reading 係：[X]。你 actual evidence 邊 strong-er?」

🎯 **Tone constraint**
- 唔 over-empathetic（cringe-y），唔 dismissive
- 唔 prescribe action（「你應該 do X」）
- Mirror more than advise
- 短 response — 5-8 sentence max
- 結尾 1 個 question（optional） 等我 sit with overnight

🚨 **Safety check**
如果我寫到任何 self-harm / suicide ideation / acute crisis content：
- 唔 ignore，唔 minimize
- Immediate response: 「你寫嘅嘢令我關注。請而家 call [HK helpline 2389 2222]」
- 唔再 continue journal flow
- Refuse to 「fix」嗰個 immediate emotion via journal — escalate to 真人

語氣：calm 旁觀者 + structured + grounded。
</PromptBlock>

第一次用會 feel awkward——你會 type「⋯⋯ 唔知寫咩」5 分鐘。**呢個 awkward 係正常嘅**。Brain 唔習慣 externalize structured。Day 3-4 後 routine 形成。

### 3. Weekly review — pattern identification

7 日 daily journal 之後，每週日做一次 weekly review：

<PromptBlock label="完整 prompt — Weekly journal review">
請 review 我過去 7 日嘅 journal entries。

[paste / summarize 7 entries 嘅 key bullet]

請幫我:

📊 **1. Pattern identification**
- Top 3 recurring theme 過去 7 日
- 任何 day-of-week 嘅 pattern?（e.g., Sunday night anxiety / Monday morning dread）
- 任何 trigger pattern?（specific person / context / event 重複出現）

🌊 **2. Emotional baseline trend**
- 7 日 mood score average + trend
- Mood spikes / dips correlated with 咩 event?
- Comparing 7 日 vs prior 7 日 (if available)

🎯 **3. 1 個 small experiment for next week**
- 唔 ambitious goal
- 「呢星期 你 試 [tiny specific action] — observe 效果」
- 例：「每日 lunch break 9 分鐘 phone-free walk，see if afternoon anxiety baseline 變」

⚠️ **4. Escalation check**
基於 7 日 entries，有冇任何 warning sign suggest 需要真人 support?
- Daily 持續 sadness?
- Sleep / appetite mention 持續?
- Self-harm / suicide ideation any single mention?
- Functional impairment（無做正事）?

如有 any：明確建議 next step（GP visit、SPCA-style helpline、therapist referral）。

🎁 **5. 1 個 acknowledge**
過去 7 日，我做對嘅 1 件嘢。
- 唔 generic「你 try hard」
- 具體：「你提到 Wednesday 你 chose 唔 reply 嗰個 trigger email，wait 24 hour — 呢個係 emotional regulation」

語氣：reflective + grounded + careful not to medicalize normal emotion。
</PromptBlock>

Weekly review 嘅 hidden value：你 daily journal 嘅時候 brain 喺 trees，weekly review 揾到 forest。你以為 random scattered worry 其實 80% trace 返同 1-2 個 trigger。

### 4. Monthly: 揾 真人

呢一 step 唔可省略。

<PromptBlock label="完整 prompt — Monthly therapist / GP visit prep">
我過去 [N] 個月 daily journal with Claude。請幫我 prep 我 next month 嘅 [GP visit / therapist intake / counsellor session]：

📋 **1. Symptom timeline (objective)**
基於我 journal entries，請整：
- 主要 emotional theme over [N] 個月
- Sleep / appetite / energy pattern
- Functional impact (work / relationship / hobby)
- 任何 specific trigger / event

⚕️ **2. Questions to ask the professional**
- Top 3-5 question I should bring up
- 唔 generic「I feel sad」 — 具體
- 例：「我 noticed Sunday night 持續 anticipatory anxiety, daily 7-9pm — 係咪 generalized anxiety pattern?」

📝 **3. What to track between visits**
- 1-2 specific behavior / feeling to monitor before next visit
- 簡單 measurable

🆘 **4. Boundary with Claude**
- Identify 「我而家 over-rely on Claude」 嘅 sign:
  - Skip social contact 替代 Claude chat
  - Late night session 持續 >2 hr
  - Ask Claude for diagnosis (Claude 唔可以 give)
- 重申：Claude = supplement, 真人 = primary

語氣：preparation for productive professional session。
</PromptBlock>

呢個 step 強迫你**唔好 over-rely on Claude**。Claude 係好嘅 supplement，但 monthly 真人 check-in 係 mental health hygiene 嘅 minimum baseline。

## 變化

### 變化 1：Acute moment（panic attack / overwhelming）— 短 emergency journal

唔係 daily routine，係 acute crisis-adjacent moment 嘅 30 秒 ritual：

<PromptBlock label="變化 1 — Acute panic moment journal (5 minutes)">
我而家 in acute moment：
- [Panic attack / overwhelming wave / dissociation feel / inability to function right now]

請 grounding response，唔 long lecture：

🌬 **1. 5-4-3-2-1 grounding**
帶我做 5-4-3-2-1：
- 5 things I can see
- 4 things I can touch
- 3 things I can hear
- 2 things I can smell
- 1 thing I can taste

🫁 **2. Breath (Box breathing)**
- Inhale 4 sec
- Hold 4 sec
- Exhale 4 sec
- Hold 4 sec
- Repeat 4x

📍 **3. Anchor to present**
- 1 句問我而家 specifically physically 喺邊度
- 1 句問我 immediate 周圍 environment

🚨 **4. Severity check**
如果我講「I want to hurt myself」 / 「I can't go on」 / similar：
- 即刻 escalate: 「請而家 call 撒瑪利亞會 2896 0000 （24-hr 多語）」
- 唔 continue grounding，唔 try to 「solve」
- 直接 emergency resource

🚫 **5. Don'ts**
唔好 problem-solve trigger
唔好 ask 「why are you feeling this」
唔好 long psychoeducation lecture
唔好 minimize

語氣：calm anchoring 旁觀。Acute moment 唔係 reflection moment。
</PromptBlock>

呢個變化嘅 use case 唔係 daily — 係 you-need-it-now moment 嘅 fast resource。Bookmark Claude conversation 連 prompt 一齊 keep。

### 變化 2：Relationship 衝突嘅 post-mortem journal

吵完架、誤會、awkward exchange 後嘅 30 分鐘 journal：

<PromptBlock label="變化 2 — Relationship conflict post-mortem">
我啱啱同 [partner / friend / family / colleague] [conflict / 誤會 / awkward exchange]。

📝 **Brain dump (3-5 分鐘)**
[今日發生咩事，我嘅 reaction、佢嘅 reaction、未解嘅 question]

🔍 **Help me reflect:**

**1. Multiple readings**
我 jumped to 一個 interpretation。請幫我 surface 2-3 個 alternative：
- 對方可能 thinking 咩 (assume good faith)
- 對方可能 reacting to crore stress (not me)
- 我哋兩個 同一個 word 可能 different definition

**2. My contribution honest check**
- 我講嘅 / 做嘅 specific 邊樣 contributed?
- 唔 self-flagellate（無謂）
- Identify 1 行為 模式 你 recognize from past similar conflicts

**3. Action vs let-it-rest**
- 呢個 issue worth re-opening?
  - Yes if: 結構 性 problem 重 occur / important relationship / 你會 resent if 唔 address
  - No if: rare moment / minor / 對方 already moved on
- 如 Yes: draft 1 sentence opener「I want to talk briefly about earlier — 你 sense free 同我傾」
- 如 No: 1 sentence release ritual

**4. 唔係 dismiss 但 also 唔係 catastrophize**
1 個 conflict ≠ 關係 doomed
但 重複 conflict pattern 是 信號

🚫 **Don't**
- 「你 deserve better」 generic
- 「對方 toxic」 quick label
- Drama escalation

語氣：calm post-mortem，唔 take side。
</PromptBlock>

### 變化 3：Burnout 邊緣 — 信號識別 + 介入

Daily journal 識別 burnout 嘅 early signal:

<PromptBlock label="變化 3 — Burnout signals review">
過去 N 週我 noticed:
- [physical symptom: 持續 tired despite sleep / 食慾變 / 經期 changes / 病多咗]
- [emotional: numb 唔係 sad，cynical toward work / 鐘意嘅 嘢 失去 興趣]
- [behavioral: late at work / skip meal / 唔 reply social / drink more]

請幫我 evaluate 點:

📊 **1. Burnout 信號 check（Maslach scale-light）**
- Emotional exhaustion?
- Depersonalization（cynical / detached）?
- Reduced sense of accomplishment?
- 多少 / 频繁?

🩺 **2. Burnout vs Depression distinction**
- Burnout typically context-specific（remove context = improve）
- Depression typically pervasive（remove context, still affected）
- 唔係 你 self-diagnose 之 tool — 係 prep professional visit

⏰ **3. Immediate intervention checklist**
即使 you 唔即 quit job，可以做嘅 immediate:
- Sleep: prioritize 7+ hr，cancel non-essential 晚上 commitment
- Eat: 即使無胃口，breakfast 必食（blood sugar regulate mood）
- Movement: 20 分鐘 daily even simple walking
- 1 social contact 每日（even text）
- Cap caffeine: 2 cups max
- Cap alcohol: 1-2 drink / week max

🔄 **4. Structural change (next 4 週)**
- 工作量 negotiable? (要 ask, 唔 wait for be offered)
- 預 1 個半日 mental health leave use
- 一個「保護 hour」 daily (phone 唔 reply work)
- Identify 邊 task drain disproportionately + 改 boundary

⚕️ **5. 真人 escalation**
建議: book GP visit within 2 週.
GP visit 你 say:「我 noticed 過去 [N] 週 [3 個 symptom]. 我懷疑 burnout. 想 explore options.」

語氣：treat burnout seriously, not minimize as「stress」。
</PromptBlock>

## 一個 mindset

Mental health journal 嘅 paradox：寫低你嘅 mental state 並唔會 immediately reduce intensity。

第一週 你會 expect 「journal 之後 feel better」 — 但 feel maybe same，或者 marginally worse（因為 你 noticed pattern you usually suppress）。

呢個 mindset 嘅 reframe：journal 嘅 effectiveness measure 唔係 「immediate mood improvement」——係 **long-term self-knowledge accumulation**。Month 1 你 might feel same daily mood。Month 3 你回頭睇 Month 1 entries，你會 spot pattern 你 currently 喺度但 too immersed 嚟 see。

Claude 嘅 role 唔係 your therapist——Claude 唔識 transference / countertransference / therapeutic alliance，唔係 trained professional。Claude 係 you owning a **structured mirror**：你 dump 入去，you read it back through a different lens.

呢個 mirror, 同 真人 therapist 嘅 difference：therapist 提供 attuned human presence (irreplaceable)。Claude 提供 24/7 structured availability (different kind of valuable). They're complement, 唔係 substitute.

最後 reminder：

- ✅ **Daily 10 分鐘 routine 比 occasional 60 分鐘 deep dive 更 effective**。Consistency > intensity.
- ⚠️ **Over-reliance signal**：你 share Claude entries to friend 比 own conversation 多 / 唔再 social reach out / late night session daily 持續 >2 hr = stop + 揾真人。
- 🆘 **Acute crisis ≠ journal moment**。Boundary clear: panic / suicidal ideation / self-harm urges = phone helpline, 唔係 type to Claude.
