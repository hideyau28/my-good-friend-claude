## 情境

你 6 月 plan 同朋友去歐洲 trip 3 個禮拜——意大利 + 法國 + 西班牙。

10 月 trip。9 月 mid 你諗：「Schengen 簽證早啲搞」。

你 Google「Schengen visa Hong Kong」，揾到法國領事館嘅 official website。連入去：

- 25 頁 PDF guideline（only English / French）
- 8 條 prerequisite list
- 4 種 application form
- VFS Global outsourcing centre 喺尖沙咀（要 book appointment）
- 「Bank statement past 3 months」 + 「proof of accommodation」 + 「return ticket」 + ⋯⋯

你 read 到第 5 頁 lost。Bank statement 要 stamp 嗎？Return ticket 要 confirmed 定 reservation OK？Travel insurance minimum coverage 幾錢？應該 apply 法國 or 西班牙 領事館？

你 close tab，諗「下星期再搞」。

下星期變兩星期。10 月 trip 變 panic — Schengen 處理時間 typically 15 working days，部分 case 30+ 日，9 月 mid 已經 cutting it close。

呢個 visa application paralysis 嘅 root cause:

- **Official 文件結構 made for 律師讀**（25 頁 PDF + cross-reference each section）
- **HK 申請人 specific need 唔 highlight**（HKSAR 護照同 BNO 嘅 process 唔同，但官網 default 寫 generic）
- **HK 申請場景 hidden cost** 唔講（VFS appointment 你以為 free 但 service fee HK$300+）
- **Reject reason 唔透明**（你 submit 你以為 done，2 週後拒簽你 don't 知 why）

呢篇用 Claude **30 分鐘 dump 你 trip details，出 personalized prep checklist** — HKSAR 持照人視角，連 application timing、文件 spec、reject 風險、appointment booking sequence 都有。

## 變化

### 變化 1：美國 ESTA（簡單但易出錯）

ESTA 嘅 form 容易但 reject 後果 嚴重：

<PromptBlock label="變化 1 — 美國 ESTA prep（visa waiver program）">
我 HKSAR passport，諗 apply 美國 ESTA 去 NYC 1 個禮拜 tourism。

請幫我 prep ESTA application：

🎯 **ESTA = Visa Waiver Program (簡單版 但唔輕視)**
- 用 official site: esta.cbp.dhs.gov（**只 用 official — 3rd party paid sites scam**）
- Cost: US$21 total（fee $4 + processing $17）
- Processing: most case 「approved」即時，部分 case 72 hour
- Validity: 2 年（multi-entry, each stay max 90 日）

📋 **Application form filling guide**

⚠️ **Critical accuracy** — ESTA 拒簽通常因為 form 錯誤：

1. **Personal info** - exactly match passport
2. **Address** - HK address with proper Chinese-to-English convention
3. **Employer** - even「retired / student」要寫
4. **Email** - 接收 approval 嘅 email，唔好用 burner address
5. **Phone** - 802 開頭 HK number 格式 +852
6. **Eligibility questions** - 9 條 yes/no question:
   - 「Do you have a physical or mental disorder?」
   - 「Are you a drug abuser?」
   - 「Have you ever been arrested?」
   - 「Have you ever been involved in espionage?」
   - 等等
   - **答 honestly** — false statement = lifetime US ban
   - 部分 question 嘅 「borderline」case 需 consult 律師 before answer

⚠️ **常見 ESTA reject reason for HK applicant**:
- Past US visa denial (要 disclose)
- Arrest record (任何 country)
- Overstay in any country (Schengen / Japan etc)
- Inconsistent employment history

⏰ **Apply timing**
- ESTA approve typically 即時，但建議 trip 前 minimum 72 小時 apply
- Rejected 嘅話：唔可以 re-apply ESTA - 要 apply 真 B1/B2 visa（complicated + interview）

🛂 **At airport**
- ESTA 唔需要印 - electronically linked to passport
- Airline 會 check ESTA status before boarding
- US immigration 仍會 question - prep visit purpose + return ticket

🚫 **唔可以 ESTA 嘅 case**
- 唔曾 visited 過 Iran / Iraq / Syria / Libya / Yemen / Somalia / Sudan / North Korea (post-2011)
- 曾被 US visa rejected
- 曾 overstay 在 US
- Dual citizenship with 部分 country

語氣：simple but precision-critical。
</PromptBlock>

### 變化 2：日本 eVisa（multi-entry / short visa-free 改變）

日本 visa 規則最近 changed：

<PromptBlock label="變化 2 — 日本 eVisa / visa-free overview for HK">
我 HKSAR passport，想去日本 2 週。請 update 我日本 visa rule (HK 持照人最新 status)。

🇯🇵 **日本 visa for HKSAR passport (2026 status)**

⚠️ **Note**: HK 護照 traditionally 90-day visa-free 入日本，但 2024-2025 出現 eVisa 推廣 / 政策 fluctuation — verify with official 日本 領事館 official site before assuming.

📋 **General visa-free 條件** (verify current)
- HKSAR passport (not 工作旅遊 visa case)
- Stay ≤ 90 日
- Tourism / 短期 business 目的
- Return ticket
- Sufficient funds proof if questioned at immigration

🆕 **eVisa programs (recent rollout)**
- 部分 country 推出 「digital visa」 program for tourism
- HK applicable? 視乎 latest 日本 政府 announcement
- Process: online apply, no embassy visit
- Use case: 多次 visit Japan 嘅 frequent tourist convenience

🎒 **Visa-free 入境注意**
即使 visa-free，仍需要:
- Return ticket
- 住宿 proof (booking confirmation)
- Sufficient funds (cash + card)
- Itinerary basic

🚨 **常見 visa-free 入境問題**
- 過去頻繁短期入境 (officer 問 「working illegally?」)
- 90 日 stay 接近 max → re-entry 同年內 risk denial
- Onward travel non-HK (e.g., Japan → 韓國 → HK) - officer 可能 question

📞 **Verify current rule before trip**
- 日本 在港領事館 official website
- 「Consulate-General of Japan in Hong Kong」 - 官方 announcement
- 唔好 rely on Reddit / FB 「I went last month no problem」 - rule can change

語氣：cautious about citing exact rule (易 outdated), 建議 verify。
</PromptBlock>

### 變化 3：學生簽證 / 工作 visa（long-stay 複雜版）

長期 visa 同 tourism visa 完全唔同 category：

<PromptBlock label="變化 3 — Student / work visa long-stay prep">
我 HKSAR passport，準備申請：[英國 student visa / 加拿大 work permit / 美國 H-1B / 澳洲 student visa]。Long-stay (>6 month) 完全唔同 short visa。

請整 long-stay visa 嘅 framework：

📐 **Long-stay vs tourism visa 差異**

| Dimension | Tourism | Student / Work |
|-----------|---------|----------------|
| Duration | < 90 日 | 1-5 年 |
| Sponsor | 自己 | University / Employer |
| Document | ~10 個 | ~30+ 個 |
| Process time | 2-4 週 | 2-6 個月 |
| Reject impact | Re-apply OK | 影響 future visa for 5+ 年 |
| Fee | $80-200 | $500-3000+ |
| Interview | rare | usually required |

📋 **Generic long-stay visa prep**

🎓 **Pre-application 階段 (3-6 個月 before)**
- Sponsor 文件 collect (admission letter / job offer / CAS)
- English test if required (IELTS / TOEFL)
- Medical examination (TB test usually)
- Financial proof (significantly more than tourism — 1-2 years cost)
- Background check certificate (HK Police certificate of no criminal conviction)

📑 **Sponsor's role**
- University：CAS (Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies) for UK / I-20 for US
- Employer：LMIA for Canada / I-129 for US / Sponsorship letter
- 你需要 follow sponsor 嘅 timeline + paperwork

🏥 **Health requirement**
- TB test (HK 認可診所)
- Vaccination records
- For some country: HIV / hepatitis screening
- Medical insurance from approved provider

💰 **Financial threshold**
- Significantly higher than tourism
- UK student：£X for 1 year living + tuition deposit
- US student：I-20 cost + sponsor financial proof
- Canada：CAD$10,000+ living + tuition
- Need 28 days continuous balance proof typically

🛂 **Visa interview prep (typically required)**
- 預期 question:
  - "Why this country?"
  - "Why this specific university / employer?"
  - "Your plan after visa expires?"
  - "Family ties in HK?"
  - "Financial sources?"
- Practice answer concise + confident
- Body language matters

📅 **Critical timeline**
- 太早 apply：visa might 過早 issue 而 trip 未開始
- 太遲 apply：miss enrollment / start date
- Sweet spot：visa issue date ~3 個月 before start date

⏰ **HK-specific complications**
- HKSAR vs BNO 同時持：揀 use 邊個 visa (consistency 重要)
- Past BNO + HKSAR mixed history: declare both
- Past mainland China stay: 部分 country requires declare

🚨 **Reject impact (long-stay specific)**
- 5-year impact on future visa applications
- 部分 country 永久 disclosure obligation
- Career impact (job offer might withdraw if visa fail)

📞 **Professional help worth considering**
- Long-stay visa worth $500-1500 immigration lawyer consult
- 特別 if past visa issue / complex case
- DIY OK if straightforward case + native speaker

語氣：long-stay = serious commitment, prep accordingly.
</PromptBlock>

## 一個 mindset

第一次申請 visa 嘅 paralysis 唔係 individual deficit——係 **bureaucratic system design 嘅 user-hostile signal**。

官方 25 頁 PDF 嘅 reader 唔係你（HKSAR first-time applicant）——係 immigration officer 同 visa lawyer。佢哋 read 嘅 fluency，係 累積咗 100 個 application 嘅 pattern recognition。你 read 同一份 PDF feel overwhelm 係正常，唔係你 stupid。

呢個 mindset 嘅 reframe：visa application 嘅 craft 唔係 「learn the system」——係 **translate 官方 system 為 your specific case 嘅 actionable steps**。Claude 嘅 role 喺呢度 critical：你 dump 你 specific situation，Claude 將 generic guideline filter 變 your personalized sequence。

最後 reminder：

- ✅ **Apply 早 + apply with margin**。Standard processing time 15 working days 嘅意思係「if everything smooth」 — 加 buffer 預 30 日 安全。
- ⚠️ **Reject 唔等於 you faulty**。Schengen reject rate for HKSAR ≈3% — 多數 reject 因 trivial document issue 可以 re-apply 修正。
- 🆘 **唔好 lie on visa application**。Even small inconsistency = 5-10 年 future visa impact。Honest about every section, even uncomfortable question.

10 月 trip，prep 從今日開始仲 just enough。Bon voyage。
