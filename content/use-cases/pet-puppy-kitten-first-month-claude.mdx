## 情境

你頭先 6pm 由 SPCA Wan Chai branch 帶 BB 返屋企。

BB 8 週大，short-hair black-and-white kitten / mixed-breed puppy。adoption paper 簽完，volunteer 同你 brief 5 分鐘，SPCA 送你一個 starter pack：少量試食、a brochure、microchip 證書、第一次 vaccination record。

你抱住 carrier 上的士，BB 喺 carrier 入面震。你住 8 樓，住緊 350 sqft 1-bedroom flat。lift lobby 嗅住消毒水味，鄰居睇你 抱住 carrier，笑下，問「咩 breed?」你答「mixed」。

入屋。

你 carefully 開 carrier 嘅 door，set 喺地下中央。等。

BB 探頭，望住個 unfamiliar 環境（4 面 vinyl floor、新 furniture smell、aircon 嘅 hum），縮返入 carrier。

你坐喺 sofa，唔知做咩好。冰箱 hum。隔離單位電視聲 muted but audible。BB 喺 carrier 入面，過咗 10 分鐘冇出嚟。

8pm 你嘗試放 food bowl 1 米外。BB 唔出嚟。

10pm 你瞓覺，BB 仲喺 carrier。

凌晨 2am 你聽到 BB cry。你 turn on phone torch，發現 BB 匿咗喺 sofa 底，眼閃閃，嘴細細 cry。

呢一刻你 panic：**我係咪 traumatize 咗 BB? 我做錯咩? 30 日內我要點 reset?**

呢篇用 Claude 拆 4 個 phase（Day 1-7 / 8-14 / 15-30 / 30 天後），每個 phase 嘅 specific milestone + warning sign。

### 3. 同 BB bond 建立 — 唔係靠抱，靠 ritual

新 owner common mistake：too much physical interaction too soon。Bonding 喺**predictability + safety**，唔係 cuddle frequency。

<PromptBlock label="完整 prompt — Trust + bond 建立 daily rituals">
我想 understand 點 build long-term trust + bond with my new BB（8 週 kitten or puppy）。我發現你建議唔好 over-cuddle 頭 2 週，但又怕「冷淡」BB。

請整 **daily bonding rituals**（5-10 分鐘 each, 高 trust ROI）：

🌅 **Morning ritual (5 mins)**
- 你起身 first task：sit on floor near BB sleeping area + silent presence
- 唔 immediate touch — let BB approach 你
- 如果 BB 過嚟：treat from hand
- Establish: you = first thing predictable + safe

🍽 **Meal-time presence**
- Sit nearby during BB eating (1-2 米距離，唔 hover)
- 唔 disturb meal — 但 BB associate your presence with positive (food)
- Same time daily — predictability is bonding

🎯 **Play ritual (15-30 mins)**
- 同一 toy daily (wand for cat, tug rope / chew for puppy)
- 同一 time daily (e.g., 7pm before dinner)
- End on win — BB 「catch」 toy, then small treat
- Don't over-stimulate — short positive sessions

🧘 **Quiet co-presence**
- 你 read / work，BB 同 room 自由
- 唔 demand attention — BB voluntarily approach over weeks
- 呢類 silent co-presence 比 active interaction 對 long-term bond stronger

🌙 **Bedtime ritual**
- 同一 phrase before sleep（"goodnight BB"）— BB learn vocal pattern + day-end cue
- Lights dim 一致 schedule — establish circadian rhythm
- 唔 force sleep in your bed — let BB 揀

🚫 **Anti-bonding actions（即使 well-intentioned）**
- 抱起 BB 走嚟走去（BB feel out of control = bonding setback）
- 用 BB 做 social media content too frequently（"propping" — over-handling）
- 多人 simultaneously approach BB（overwhelming）
- 喺 BB hiding spot 強拉 BB out
- Inconsistent schedule（today 7am feed, tomorrow 9am — destabilizing）

🔬 **Bonding signals BB shows you（progressive）**:

Week 1:
- 開始 voluntarily come out from hiding when you enter room
- Eat food while you sit nearby

Week 2:
- Approach you for treat (not just food)
- Briefly lean against you / 過嚟 touch your leg

Week 3:
- Sleeping in your visible area (not hidden)
- Belly exposure during rest (high trust sign)
- Slow blink (cat)
- Tail wag relaxed (puppy)

Week 4+:
- Voluntarily seeks physical contact
- Greets you at door
- Vocalizes for attention
- Sleeps near you

語氣：patience-emphasized。Bonding 唔係 weekly milestone — 係 multi-year compound。Year 5 你哋嘅 bond 唔係 year 1 嘅 base + 4 年延續 — 係完全不同 quality.
</PromptBlock>

### 4. Crisis management — Day 1-7 常見問題

新 owner 頭 7 日通常遇到 1-3 個 panic moments。Claude help triage：

<PromptBlock label="完整 prompt — Common Day 1-7 issues triage">
我新 BB（8 週）住咗 [幾日]。出現以下 issue [描述]。

請 help me triage:

📋 **常見 Issue 1: BB 唔食 12-24 hr**

Triage:
- 少於 12 hr: Normal stress reaction, monitor + offer high-value food (small piece churu for cat / cooked chicken for puppy)
- 12-24 hr: Try food variety + warm slightly (release smell) + hand feed if needed
- >24 hr (kitten / puppy): VET — small animals dehydrate / hypoglycemia 快
- 任何時候 + lethargic / vomiting / diarrhea: VET immediately

📋 **常見 Issue 2: BB 持續 hide + cry**

Triage:
- Day 1-3: Normal acclimation, do NOT force out, 確保 food / water / litter accessible near hiding spot
- Day 4-7: 仲完全 hide = your presence pattern 可能太 intense, 反而 give more solitude
- 加 night cry: 可能 missing siblings (8 週 BB 剛離開 litter) — 加 ticking clock under blanket (mimics heartbeat) + warm hot water bottle wrapped in towel
- Cry + 唔食 + lethargic = VET

📋 **常見 Issue 3: Litter / potty 唔成功**

對於 kitten:
- Place BB in litter after meals + waking
- 確認 box size + litter type same as source
- 多 box if large apartment (Cat: N+1 box for N cats)
- 唔好 punish accidents — clean with enzyme cleaner (prevents repeat)
- 持續 >Day 5 唔 use: medical issue check (UTI common in young kittens)

對於 puppy:
- 8 週 puppy bladder very small — 每 1-2 hr potty trip
- After meals + waking + play: immediate to pee pad
- 唔好 puppy free-roam too soon — accidents = setback
- 持續 >Week 2 唔 progress: behavioral consultation

📋 **常見 Issue 4: BB 咬 / 抓 你**

Triage:
- Play biting normal — but redirect to toy immediately
- 唔好 hand-as-toy 訓練 BB (long-term problem)
- High-pitched「ouch」+ ignore 1 minute (mimics littermate teaching)
- Persistent aggression beyond play: behavioral consultation (rare in 8 週 — usually under-socialization)

📋 **常見 Issue 5: BB 同 partner / family member 不熟**

Triage:
- BB 通常 first bond with primary caregiver (the one feeding most)
- Partner / family slowly join via:
  - Sit silently in room (no interaction demand)
  - Hand treat once trust builds
  - Take over 1 feeding session per day
- Don't 強迫 BB interact with stranger - lifelong fearful adult

📋 **常見 Issue 6: 鄰居 complaint (puppy 持續 cry)**

Triage:
- 8 週 puppy 頭幾晚 cry normal (missing litter)
- Solutions:
  - Crate near your bed first 2 週 (proximity reassurance)
  - Heartbeat clock + warm pad
  - 短期 anti-anxiety pheromone diffuser (Adaptil) HK$300
- Communicate proactively with direct neighbors: knock + explain + apologize + timeline
- 持續 >2 週: vet consult (separation anxiety meds short-term + behavioral plan)

🚨 **Vet immediately signals (Day 1-7)**:
- 持續 vomiting >2 episodes / 8 hr
- Diarrhea + blood
- Refused food + water >24 hr
- Visible parasites
- Lethargic + 唔玩 + 唔 respond to stimuli
- Difficulty breathing
- Limping suddenly

語氣：reassuring but not dismissive — 第一次 owner 嘅 concern valid，但唔係每個 anxiety = emergency。
</PromptBlock>

## 變化

### 變化 1：領養 senior pet（5 歲以上）

Senior pet adoption 嘅 dynamics 完全唔同：

<PromptBlock label="變化 1 — Senior pet (5+) adoption onboarding">
我領養 [5 歲 / 8 歲 / 10 歲+] 嘅 [species]，源自 [SPCA / surrender / rescue]。Adult pet 唔係 8 週 kitten — 完全唔同 approach。

請整 senior-specific 30 天 onboarding：

🐾 **Senior adopt 嘅獨特 challenge**
- 已有 personality + habit pattern（possibly trauma history）
- 健康 baseline 未知（不像 kitten/puppy 平均健康）
- Adaptation 慢過 young pet（established neural patterns）
- 部分 senior pet 一世只 bond with 一個 caregiver

🗓 **Phase 1 — Day 1-7：Slow exposure**
- BB 自己揀 pace — 真係自己揀（部分 senior pet 頭週完全 hide normal）
- 唔需要 daily「playful」interaction — senior pet 可能 prefer quiet
- Maintain previous routine if known (rescue 可能 keep record)

🗓 **Phase 2 — Day 8-14：Health baseline**
- 第一次 vet visit Day 7-10:
  - Full blood panel (senior baseline)
  - Dental check (常見 senior issue)
  - Arthritis assessment
  - Vaccination history reconcile
- Diet review: switch to senior formula if not already

🗓 **Phase 3 — Day 15-30：Personality emerge**
- 部分 trauma signs surface 喺 Week 3-4 (not Week 1)
- Identify trigger pattern (specific noise / object / behavior)
- Behavioral plan if needed (consult cat / dog behaviorist)
- Bond 進度 slower than kitten — Year 1 才真正「settle in」

⚠️ **Adjust expectation**:
- Day 30 review: senior pet 可能仲 cautious — normal
- 健康 issue may emerge first 3-6 個月 — financial buffer 重要
- 鐘意 senior pet 嘅 owner share 一個 trait: patience + non-comparison

語氣：respectful for senior pet's life history，唔好 try to「fix」existing personality。
</PromptBlock>

### 變化 2：已有 resident pet — Second pet introduction

如果你屋企有現有 pet，新加入需要 careful introduction：

<PromptBlock label="變化 2 — Second pet 介紹 protocol">
我屋企有 [resident pet — species + age + temperament]。我即將 adopt [new pet — same / different species + age]。

請整 **safe introduction protocol** (avoid common mistake — too fast = lifelong tension)：

🗓 **Phase 1 — Week 1: No visual contact**
- New pet in separate room, door closed
- 唔好 face-to-face yet
- Scent swap daily:
  - Swap blanket / bed between rooms
  - Wipe each pet's cheek with same cloth, present to other
- Resident pet routine UNCHANGED — 唔好因為新嚟者 disrupt resident

🗓 **Phase 2 — Week 2: Door-cracked / barrier introduction**
- Door cracked open or baby gate
- Both pets see each other briefly + treat reward
- 短期 sessions (5 mins x 2-3 / day)
- 唔 force interaction — observe body language

🗓 **Phase 3 — Week 3: Supervised same-room**
- Both in same room, both leashed / new pet in carrier
- 5-10 mins at a time, treats throughout
- 任何 hissing / lunging = back to Phase 2 next session
- Resident pet's POV: 確認 you not「neglecting」 — 1-on-1 time with resident daily

🗓 **Phase 4 — Week 4+: Slow free interaction**
- Supervised free interaction
- Separate feeding stations (food guarding common conflict source)
- Separate resting spots (each pet has own safe zone)
- Resident pet preferential treatment maintained (you ate first, you greeted first)

🚩 **Red flags — slow down protocol**:
- Resident pet 唔食 / withdraws
- Resident pet aggressive
- New pet shows extreme stress (constant hiding even >Day 14)
- Resident pet starts marking / spraying (territorial response)

🎯 **Long-term success**:
- 大部分 properly introduced pet 6-12 個月成 friendly companions
- 部分 (about 20%) tolerate but never close - acceptable outcome
- 極少數 (少於 5%) cannot coexist - rehome consideration after 6 個月 honest attempt

語氣：patience-emphasized。Wrong intro = years of tension. Done right = lifelong companions.
</PromptBlock>

### 變化 3：街上撿到嘅 pet — Rescue 從零

街上撿到 pet 嘅 onboarding 完全唔同 — health unknown + history unknown：

<PromptBlock label="變化 3 — 街上 rescue pet 處理">
我喺街上 [區] 撿到一隻 [估計年齡 + species]。狀況: [瘦 / 受傷 / friendly / 驚 / matted / 跳蚤可見]。我想 keep 但首先要 stabilize。

請整 immediate rescue protocol：

⚠️ **Hour 0-24：Stabilize + assess**

- Safety first:
  - 用 carrier / 紙箱 transport (唔好 直接抱 — 街 pet 可能 panic bite / scratch)
  - 戴 thick towel as barrier
  - 唔好接近 visibly aggressive / 受傷 嘅 pet — call SPCA (2802 0501) for assistance

- Immediate medical:
  - 即去 vet (24-hr emergency if 嚴重 dehydrated / 受傷)
  - 同 vet brief: street rescue, unknown history
  - First check:
    - Microchip scan (可能 someone's lost pet, not stray)
    - Parasites (fleas, ticks, worms — almost certain in street pet)
    - Wounds / infections
    - FIV / FeLV test (cat) / heartworm test (dog)
    - Vaccination start

- Isolation:
  - 如有 existing pet, KEEP STREET RESCUE COMPLETELY SEPARATE first 2 週 (disease prevention)
  - Quarantine in bathroom / 細房間 with own supplies
  - Wash hands between handling

🏛 **HK 法律 reality**
- 狗：強制 chip + license — scan chip 即知 owner. 冇 chip 嘅 stray dog 撿到須報 AFCD (1823) 24 hr 內
- 貓: 唔強制 chip，但建議仍 report to AFCD lost pet database — 可能有 owner 揾緊

🗓 **Day 1-14: 健康 stabilize + check ownership claim**

- AFCD wait period: 通常 4-7 days for chip lookup + posting public lost pet board
- 期間 you can foster (不 own — 律師 perspective)
- 同時:
  - Treat parasites
  - Vaccination series start

## 一個 mindset

你 adopt BB 嗰一刻，你 simultaneously do 兩樣事:

**1. 你 commit 一個 7-15 年 caretaking relationship**。BB 嘅 lifespan 大部分時間你哋會係 daily presence 喺對方 life。
**2. 你 enter 一個 pre-existing being 嘅 narrative**——BB 8 週前已經有 personality genetic + 母親 nurturing + littermate dynamics 嘅 first 8 weeks experience。

呢個 mindset 嘅 reframe：你嘅 30 日 onboarding plan 唔係「training BB」——係**你同 BB 互相 learn 嘅 first chapter**。

BB 學識你嘅 voice、你嘅 schedule、你 sit on sofa 嘅哪個 spot 係 favourite。同時你學識 BB 嘅 tail flick 何時 means 興奮 vs frustration、佢 hide 何時 normal vs concerning、佢 silence 何時 calm vs withdrawn。

呢個 mutual learning curve **未來 14 年都 ongoing**——你 Year 5 仲會 surprise BB shows new trick。

最後 reminder：

- ✅ **Day 1 唔 perfect 唔 representative**。第一晚 BB cry 唔係 sign 你 failed — 係 sign BB 經歷 huge transition。
- ⚠️ **唔好 compare 你嘅 BB 同 social media 嘅 「instantly cuddle」 BB**。Camera 唔 show Week 3 嘅 vet visit / Week 4 嘅 accidents。
- 🆘 **Day 30 仲 struggling = call vet + 唔係 ashamed**。Adoption return rate 喺 honest discussion 之下 manageable。BB 應該 thrive with right family — not necessarily yours, and that's OK.

歡迎 BB 嚟到你 屋企。下一個 14 年精彩開始。
