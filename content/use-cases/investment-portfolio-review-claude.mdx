## 情境

- US stock (VOO + QQQ): HK$300k (30%)

5 年 later, today review broker statement:
- US stock: **HK$720k (51%)** (S&P 500 ran up 20-25% annually 過去 5 年, 加 contributions)
- HK stock: **HK$280k (20%)** (HSI flat 5 年, slight loss)
- Bond: **HK$320k (23%)** (slight growth)
- Cash: **HK$80k (6%)** (drift down from spending)
- **Total: HK$1.4m**

你 brain immediate observation:
- **US weighting up to 51%** (target 30%): concentration risk
- **HK stock down to 20%** (target 30%): underweight
- **Bond at 23%** (target 30%): slightly underweight
- **Cash 6%** (target 10%): below target

你 mental check:
- 5 年無 rebalance 累積 51% US 「concentration」 — if US correct 30%, your portfolio drop 15%
- HK stock under-allocation: missing potential rebound
- Bond under: less defensive cushion
- Cash thin: emergency fund / dry powder limited

呢個 portfolio drift 普 universal:

- **「Buy + hold」 winning mantra 真實** — but 5 年 nothing pricing change reactive
- **Volatility = 「opportunity」 OR 「risk」** depending position
- **Tax / fee impact rebalance** — 顯 著 但 frequently ignored
- **Emotional bias hold winners** — 不 sell 「if it keeps running」

呢篇用 Claude **systematic portfolio review + concentration risk identification + actionable rebalance plan**.

## 變化

### 變化 1：個 別 stock (single stock) 重 position

如 你 有 個別 stock 大 weighting:

<PromptBlock label="變化 1 — 個別 stock 大 position management">
我 individual stock [Apple / Tesla / Tencent / etc.] 大 position (>10% portfolio).

📐 **Single stock concentration risk**

🚨 **Inherent risk**
- 1 company specific: bankruptcy / fraud / disruption / 規 change
- Different from index (which diversifies away single-stock risk)
- Famous historical: GM, Enron, AIG (mighty fall)

🎯 **Position size guidance**
- < 5% portfolio: low risk
- 5-10%: moderate, watch for life event
- > 10%: concentration risk emerging
- > 20%: 大 risk, immediate review
- > 30%: 警 must reduce

📊 **Review framework**

✅ **Each large position**:
- Why originally bought?
- 持 holding period (cost basis)
- Current valuation vs reasonable target
- Concentration risk acceptable?
- Tax implication sale (HK 沒 tax)

✅ **Trim 部分 vs hold all**:
- Trim 50% if concentration > 10%: 雙重 benefit (still upside + reduced risk)
- Sell 100% if conviction lost
- Stay full position only if conviction strong + 不 over portfolio %

📋 **Trim plan**:
- Don't 1-day all-out (market timing risk)
- Sell 10-25% portion at a time
- Spread over 2-4 weeks
- Tax-efficient sequencing (HK 沒 cap gains, simpler)

🎯 **Reinvest sale proceeds**:
- ETF / index (broader diversification)
- 同 sector ETF (XLK if Apple sale, etc.)
- International exposure
- Bond for stability if portfolio too 重 equity

🚨 **Don'ts**:
- Hold concentrated 「I know this company best」 (familiarity bias)
- Sell entire position on news headline
- Average down on losing position (catching falling knife)
- Pyramid up on winners (compound concentration)

語氣：respect individual investor conviction + risk management.
</PromptBlock>

## 一個 mindset

Portfolio rebalance 嘅 paradox: **「Set and forget」 is conventional advice — but 5 年 set + forget = drift to concentration**.

呢個 paradox resolution: 「Set」 = original allocation. 「Forget」 = ignore daily noise. 「Rebalance」 = annual systematic check, not abandonment. 「Forget」 ≠ 「permanently ignore」.

呢個 mindset 嘅 reframe: Portfolio review 不 「constant tweaking」 嘅 anxiety, 唔 「complete abandonment」 嘅 negligence. Annual structured 1 hour review = sweet spot.

Claude 嘅 role 喺 portfolio: 你 alone reading market news + opinion piece 容易 emotional react. Claude container 提供 data-driven distance — 你 dump portfolio + concerns, Claude structure analysis + option. Decision 後續 你 own (IFA potentially involve large amount).

最後 reminder：

- ✅ **不 perfect portfolio = better than no review**. Year 1 some change > 5 year drift compound.
- ⚠️ **HK 沒 cap gains tax**: rebalance freely 比 大部分 markets 容易. Use this advantage.
- 🆘 **大 portfolio (HK$1m+) + complex situation**: IFA consult reasonable. Claude 1 input, 不 sole basis.

明日 weekend, 1 hour portfolio review. 5 年 drift 識別. 4 week rebalance execute. 持續 yearly cycle from here.
