## 情境

5 月 13 日 sunday brunch, 你同 partner cafe 計 financial situation.

Last month partner Jenny 嘅 contract job (旅遊 industry) suddenly terminated. 4 weeks notice. End date: April 30.

Today: 13 May. Jenny job search 13 日, no offer yet.

你哋 financial:
- Combined monthly take-home: was HK$95k, now HK$60k (你 only)
- Monthly fixed expense: HK$55k (mortgage HK$22k + 細路學費 HK$8k + insurance HK$4k + 家庭基本 HK$15k + utilities HK$6k)
- Savings: 你 saving account HK$50k + 你哋 共同 saving HK$38k = HK$88k
- Investment: HK$320k diversified (但 不 想 liquidate 短期)
- Credit card: HK$15k balance (typical monthly cycle)

你 brain mental calc:
- HK$60k income - HK$55k expense = HK$5k surplus only
- 不 acceptable cushion + 細路 emergency = thin

Partner: 「If 我 唔 揾 job 3 個月內, 我哋 dip 到 saving big.」

你: 「咁 our 「emergency fund」 actually OK 嗎?」

Partner: 「我 哋 從 未 計過. 你?」

你: 「冇. 」

呢個 emergency fund 嘅 paradox:

- **「儲 HK$50k 「覺」 OK」** — but never 真 calc 真正 expense base
- **「3-6 個月 expense」 conventional wisdom** — but 邊個 expense base?
- **「Saving account 隨 取」** — but 1% interest = inflation lose
- **「Investment 流動」** — but 30% drop 不 ideal sell timing
- **「Family income duplicate」** — but 突發 collective layoff 不 hypothetical

What 你 actually need: **specific calc for 你 family + 邊度 stash decision + 3 個月 build plan**.

呢篇用 Claude 20 分鐘 **有系統嘅 emergency fund calc + 邊度 stash + execution plan**.

## 一個 mindset

Emergency fund 嘅 paradox: **你 hope 你 唔 use it. 但越 hope 不 use, 越 critical to have it ready.**

呢個 paradox 嘅 reason: 「Used emergency fund」 = adverse event 已 happen = catastrophic outcome avoided. 「Unused emergency fund」 = quiet confidence + sleep well.

呢個 mindset 嘅 reframe: Emergency fund 唔係 「sitting idle waste」. Emergency fund 係 **「peace of mind paid in cash」**. The opportunity cost (lower return vs equity) **是 「permission slip for life crisis」**.

Claude 嘅 role: 你 alone do 「emergency fund 我應該幾多」 讀行貨 blog 信 「6 個月」. Specific 你 family situation 不同。Claude 容你 dump complete picture, output specific number + tier structure + product. Decision-implementation gap 縮短.

最後 reminder：

- ✅ **「Some emergency fund」 > 「Perfect amount eventually」**. Start small (1 個月 expense), scale up over 12 個月.
- ⚠️ **不 confuse 「emergency fund」 同 「saving for goal」**. Travel fund / car fund / 細路 college fund are separate.
- 🆘 **使用 emergency 不 failure**. 使 it for real emergency, replenish, life continues.
