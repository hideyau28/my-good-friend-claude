## 情境

5 月 23 日 8:47pm，你 receive MPF Q1 statement.

你打 開 PDF:
- **HSBC Mandatory Provident Fund — SuperTrust Plus**
- Member: 你
- Account opened: April 2016 (10 年)
- Current portfolio value: **HK$420,358**
- Year-to-date return: -2.3%
- 1-year return: 5.8%
- 5-year annualized: 4.1%

你 default 反應: 「OK 啦, growing.」

但你想起 lunch break 同事 Stephen (同 age, 同 industry, 同 employer 10 年):
- Stephen MPF portfolio value: **HK$680k**
- Same 月份 contribute amount
- 同 employer match
- 同 starting date

你 internal: 「點解 26 萬 difference?」

你 ask Stephen lunch:
- 「邊個 fund?」
- Stephen: 「我 開頭 default fund 1 年, 然後 switch 全 equity. 30 歲 high risk OK.」
- 你: 「⋯」 (沒 ever switched)
- Stephen: 「Default fund return 4% / yr. Equity return 8-9% / yr historical. 10 yr compound difference: big.」
- 你: 「Claude, please help me.」

呢個 MPF mistake universal HK pattern:

- **78% MPF account holders 從 未 switched fund** (data MPFA 2024)
- **Default fund 通常 「DIS」 Default Investment Strategy** — 60/40 mixed but high fee
- **「Set and forget」 mentality** — 10 年 unchanged
- **「我 唔識 投資」 fear** — Claude 容易 help education
- **Compound effect** — 1% fee difference over 30 年 = 25% retirement gap

呢篇用 Claude 30 分鐘 **有系統 MPF review** — 拆 fund performance、 fee impact、age-appropriate strategy、actionable 下個月 switch decision.

⚠️ **Disclaimer 重複**: Claude 唔係 IFA. 大 amount switch decision 適合 IFA / MPF intermediary 同行.

20s applicant 不同 considerations:

我 25 歲, MPF 開 2 年, 余額 HK$50k.

📐 **20s MPF special considerations**

🎯 **Aggressive allocation appropriate**
- 40 年 horizon to retirement
- Equity exposure 100% reasonable
- 「Recovery time」 even if 30% drop
- Time on side maximally

📊 **Fund selection**
- 100% global equity index OR diversified equity mix
- Lower fee priority (you're paying 30+ years)
- Avoid balanced fund default — too conservative for age

💰 **Contribution maximize**
- 公司 match 100% even if minimum 5%

- 月 transfer to current employer scheme OR keep at prior
- Transferring consolidate (easier tracking)

🎯 **30 年 projection**
HK$50k current + HK$5k monthly contribution + 7% real return:
- Year 30: ~HK$6m

### 變化 2：接近退休 (55+)

接近 retirement de-risk strategy:

<PromptBlock label="變化 2 — Approaching retirement (55+) MPF strategy">
我 56 歲, 9 年 to retire. 余額 HK$1.5m. 想 de-risk.

📐 **55+ considerations**

🎯 **Glide path de-risk**
- 「Lifecycle fund」 (DIS or scheme-specific): 自動 de-risk by age
- 自己 rebalance:
  - 55 歲: 60% equity / 40% bond
  - 60 歲: 40% equity / 60% bond
  - 65 歲 onwards: 30% equity / 70% bond (still some growth)

💰 **Withdrawal planning**
- 65 歲: lump sum or annuity option
- Lump sum: invest yourself / 個別 annuity / fixed deposit
- Annuity (HKMC Annuity Plan): guaranteed monthly income for life
- Mix often optimal

📊 **Tax considerations**
- MPF withdrawal at 65: not taxable (after 1 year wait usually)
- Re-invest in dividend stock / fixed deposit: dividend tax-free HK
- Foreign investment: tax in source country may apply

⏰ **Re-employ after 65**
- Continue contribute if working
- Tax-deductible VC continues
- 不 force withdrawal mandatory

🏥 **Healthcare considerations**
- Healthcare inflation HK 顯著
- 預 medical expenses post-retirement
- 私人 medical insurance even post-retirement

🎒 **Other retirement income**
- Property (rent / sale)
- Investment portfolio
- 公 social benefits (老人 CSSA if applicable)
- Family support (Asian context common)

📋 **5-year-out planning**
- IFA / wealth manager consult important
- Estate planning + will
- Beneficiary designation update
- Power of attorney prep

🚨 **Late-career pitfall**
- 過 aggressive close to retirement (no recovery time)
- 過 conservative early (don't grow enough)
- 不 plan withdrawal phase
- Ignore inflation impact

語氣：transition planning, balance growth and preservation.
</PromptBlock>

### 變化 3：自僱 / freelance 不 employer-matched MPF

自僱嘅 MPF complexity 同 reality:

<PromptBlock label="變化 3 — Self-employed / freelance MPF">
我自僱 freelance, 5 年. MPF 不 same employer match. Want maximize.

📐 **Self-employed MPF specifics**

🎯 **Mandatory contribution**
- Self-employed 加 MPF mandatory rate: 5% relevant income
- Income above HK$30k / month: full 5%
- Income below HK$7,100 / month: optional contribute
- 月 income variable: contribute based on actual income

💰 **No employer match**
- 不 「matching」 contribution like employed
- 但 tax deductible (HK$18k / year cap mandatory)
- Voluntary contribution: additional HK$60k / year tax-deductible

📊 **Scheme selection**
- 自僱 揀 own scheme (any approved provider)
- Lower fee 揀 比 important (no employer baseline cushion)
- DIY 不 default fund

⏰ **Income volatility management**
- 月 income low: contribute minimum but stay enrolled
- 月 income 大: voluntary contribution maximize tax saving
- 不 stop contribute even rough months (continuous record)

🎯 **Strategy**

✅ **Year 1**:
- Enroll appropriate scheme
- Voluntary contribution start mid-year if cash flow OK

✅ **Year 2+**:
- Maximize voluntary contribution if income strong
- Tax saving 18% bracket = HK$18k tax-deductible save HK$3.24k tax / year
- Mandatory 5% on capped HK$30k income = HK$1500 / month

⏰ **Tax filing**
- Self-employed file tax: MPF contribution deductible
- Voluntary contribution receipt save
- Tax efficient timing

🚨 **Self-employed pitfall**
- Skip contribution because 月 income variable
- 不 enroll thinking 「I'll start later」
- Voluntary skipped because 「we'll do it」

📋 **Annual review**
- Income projection
- Tax-efficient contribution
- Scheme performance

語氣：self-employment 不 excuse skip retirement planning.
</PromptBlock>

## 一個 mindset

MPF review 嘅 hidden truth: **「未 review」 嘅 cost 比 「 review 後 wrong decision」 嘅 cost 大 5-10 倍**.

呢個 hidden truth's reason:
- 「Wrong decision」 = 1-2% sub-optimal return 1 year
- 「Never review」 = 1.7% sub-optimal return compound over 30 年 = significant cumulative
- 1.7% / yr × 30 年 ≈ 65% portfolio difference at retirement

呢個 mindset 嘅 reframe: MPF 唔係 「retirement 嗰 part 我 default 唔 manage」 — 係 **30 年複利機器 deserve 30 分鐘 / year attention**.

Claude 嘅 role 喺 MPF: 你 alone navigate jargon (alpha, expense ratio, glide path) 困難 + sense intimidation. Claude container 提供 explanatory + quantitative framework. 你 dump statement + concern, Claude reflect specific number + option. Decision 後續 你 own (IFA potentially involve).

最後 reminder：

- ✅ **78% HK 員工 not switched fund**. You doing review = top 22% — already ahead.
- ⚠️ **不 over-react single year**. 1 yr return high variance. 3-5 yr compare meaningful.
- 🆘 **大 amount switch (>HK$500k)** consider IFA consultation. Claude 不 replace licensed adviser.

下個月 first review session. Year-end check progress. 5 年 later compounding visibly different.
