## 情境

12 月 20 日，你最後一日 Tokyo trip。

成 trip 你買咗：

- Day 1：Don Quijote 化妝品 + skincare ¥18,500
- Day 2：UNIQLO + GU 衣 ¥12,000

- Day 4：百貨公司 LV passport holder ¥38,000
- Day 5：朝早 Akihabara 漫畫 + 玩具 ¥8,500

Total ¥122,000，HK$ 約 6,200。

退稅 10% 大約 ¥12,200 = HK$620。**值得拎**。

你 11am 朝早抵 Haneda 機場，13:00 飛 HK。

11:15am: check-in 前過 customs tax-refund counter。

你拎出張包好嘅 LV passport holder。Customs officer:

> 「Receipt please」

你 paper bag 裏面有 4-5 張 receipt mixed 喺 luggage。你 dig 出嚟。

你揾。

15 分鐘過去。

> 「Don Quijote 嘅 tax-free 程序 你 already 喺 store 入面 done sales tax exemption — 呢張 receipt 有 stamp。你唔需要喺 機場再 process。」

你：「等等⋯ 即係 Don Quijote 化妝品 ¥18,500 嘅 8% tax exemption already discounted at store?」

> 「Yes」

OK。你 confused 一陣。

> 「LV 嘅 receipt please」

你拎出 LV receipt 同 passport holder.

> 「呢個 case 已經 stamp by 百貨店 — 你已經 paid tax-included price，呢度 refund counter 唔處理。」

更 confused 你。「咁⋯⋯邊啲 我可以 refund?」

呢個 retail-tourist-tax confusion 嘅 root cause:

- **日本 tax-free system 有 2 種 process**（store-level immediate vs airport later — 你以為要 always airport）

- **Item type qualifies 唔同**（liquid / consumable vs general goods 分類）

## 跟住做

### 1. Trip 前 understand tax refund 基本

開 Claude，先 understand 你 trip 嗰個地方嘅 tax system：

<PromptBlock label="完整 prompt — 日本 tax-free system 大致 understanding">
我即將去日本 [N 日] trip，預算購物 [HK$ amount]。我從未做過 tax refund。

請整 **日本 tax-free system overview** for HK 遊客：

🇯🇵 **日本 consumption tax (消費稅)**
- Rate: 10% 標準 / 8% on 食物 + drinks
- Tourists 持非 日本 passport stay < 6 個月 = qualified for tax exemption

📋 **2 種 process** (重要 distinguish):

**Type A: Store-level immediate exemption** (大部分 case)
- Don Quijote / Bic Camera / 大型 chain store
- 你 出示 passport 喺 cashier
- 即時 deduct tax = 你 pay tax-exempt price
- Receipt + passport stamped at store
- **完成 — 唔需要再 機場 process**
- 你 拎走 嘅 「consumable」 sealed 唔可以開 (chocolate / 化妝品 / health products)

**Type B: Airport-level refund** (少數 case)
- 你 喺 store 已 pay tax-included price
- Receipt 留低
- 機場 customs counter present receipt + item
- Tax refund as cash / credit card credit

**Most stores 而家 use Type A**, so 你 trip 中 99% 唔用機場 refund process。

📏 **Threshold + restrictions**

✅ **General goods (一般商品)** - 電子 / 衣服 / 包 / 飾物
- Minimum: 同一日同一店 ¥5,000+
- Maximum: 無 limit
- 可以開 / 立即用

✅ **Consumables (消耗品)** - 化妝品 / 食物 / 飲品 / 健康用品
- Minimum: 同一日同一店 ¥5,000+
- Maximum: 同一日同一店 ¥500,000
- **必須 sealed 不開** until depart 日本
- Customs 可能 抽查 — 唔可以 use / consume 喺 日本

❌ **Mix 唔可以**:
- 同一 receipt 嘅 general + consumable 唔可以一齊算 threshold
- e.g., ¥3,000 衣服 + ¥3,000 化妝品 = 唔 qualify 任何一邊 ¥5,000 threshold

📝 **必要 documents**
- 護照 original (NOT copy)
- 你 wear 「同名 passport」 嘅 fact 對 cashier
- Customs declaration form (在某店要填) — store 提供
- Receipt: 不可丟，亦不可 fold (機場可能 verify)

🏷 **Items NOT qualified for tax exemption**
- 食 / 喝 飲品 in store immediately
- Travel-related expense (酒店 / transport / restaurant meal)
- 二手 / 古董 (部分案例)
- 已 ship to HK 嘅 (you must take with you)

🏬 **常見 chain store + their process**:
- Don Quijote: Type A (store-level, fast)
- Bic Camera / Yamada Denki: Type A
- UNIQLO: Type A (NOT 全部店, 只 select branches handle tax-free)
- 高 end 百貨 (Mitsukoshi, Isetan, Takashimaya): Type B (上 4F customer service 處理)
- Sezon / Daimaru: mostly Type B
- 小 boutique / 街市 / vending machine: 不 handle tax-free (你 pay tax-include)

💰 **預期 saving**
- 10% on general goods (你 pay 90.9% of 標 price)
- 8% on consumables (你 pay 92.6% of 標 price)
- HK$10,000 spending = ~HK$1,000 saved
- Worth time investment

語氣：clear distinction between 2 process types。
</PromptBlock>

Claude clear 度，你最 important takeaway：**大部分 case 已 store-level 處理 — 唔需要 airport stress**。

### 3. 機場 final claim sequence (Type B refund items)

最後一日 機場 prep，避免 boarding-rush panic：

<PromptBlock label="完整 prompt — 機場 tax refund claim final sequence">
我 Day 5 飛 HK，13:00 boarding。我有 [N 張 Type B refund receipt = pending ¥XXX]。

請整 **機場 final claim sequence**：

⏰ **Timing strategy**

**Arrive 機場**: 預 11:00 (boarding 13:00, 2 hour buffer)
- 1 hour for tax refund process
- 30 min check-in + security
- 30 min boarding + walk to gate

**Sequence**:
1. **Step 1: 到達 機場 (11:00)**
   - 揾 「Tax Refund」 sign / customs counter
   - 通常 international departure 之前 (after check-in)
   - 在 Tokyo 機場: Haneda T2 / T3, Narita T1 / T2 都有

2. **Step 2: Queue at customs counter (11:00 - 11:30)**
   - Peak hour avoid (early morning / late evening 通常 quicker)
   - 大旺 季節 (春節 / 黃金週) 排 1+ hr 可能

3. **Step 3: Document check (11:30 - 11:45)**
   - Officer ask:
     - Passport
     - Boarding pass
     - Receipts (organized by store)
     - Items (must show some)
   - Officer stamp receipts after verify
   - **Critical**: items must be unopened (esp consumables)

4. **Step 4: Cash refund or credit (11:45 - 12:00)**
   - 揀 cash refund (¥ 即收) 或 credit card refund (slower, 2-6 週)
   - Cash 通常 marginally less ($1-2 service charge per transaction)
   - Credit card refund original card

5. **Step 5: Check-in + security + boarding (12:00 - 12:45)**

📋 **必要 prep before 機場**

- [ ] All receipts collected + sorted (Type B priority)
- [ ] Consumable items sealed (in carry-on or luggage)
- [ ] Passport handy
- [ ] Boarding pass print / phone
- [ ] Calculator / notes app ready (verify refund amount)

🎒 **Pack strategy**

Consumable items (化妝品 / chocolate / health food) sealed:
- 留 carry-on if 收 size （customs 多數要求 show item）
- 在 check-in luggage 都 OK 如 declared properly
- Best: 30% in carry-on (sample for show) + 70% in check-in

General goods (電子 / 包 / 衣):
- 不 mandatory show, but 有時 officer ask
- Both carry-on + check-in OK

🚨 **Common reject reason** (你要 avoid)

- ❌ Receipt 不全 / 丟 — irrecoverable
- ❌ Consumable opened / used in Japan
- ❌ Item not present (already used)
- ❌ Passport 沒帶 (officer cannot verify)
- ❌ Boarding pass for connecting flight 不 sufficient (must show 直接出 日本 嘅 flight)
- ❌ Over 6 個月 stay = NOT qualified tourist (not your case)

⏰ **If queue long + boarding tight**

- Skip refund if amount < HK$200 + < 30 min boarding time
- Math: HK$200 refund vs missed flight HK$1000+ rebook
- 嘅 trade-off 唔 worth

🆘 **Backup if 拎唔到 refund**

- 部分 refund 可以 post-trip claim via Global Blue website (歐洲 case)
- 日本 case: typically 機場 only, post-trip rare
- 部分 store 有 「Mail-in refund」 program — check store-level

💰 **Refund expectation**

- Cash refund: 即攞 ¥ instant
- Credit card refund: appears 2-6 週後
- 唔 expect refund 全 saved ¥ (handling fee ¥ 50-200 typical)

📞 **If issue**:
- Customs officer 唔肯 stamp → ask supervisor calmly
- 完全 refund refused → ask written 拒簽 reason
- Receipt error → return to original store if time (within 24 hr 通常 OK)

語氣：efficient + buffered for queue uncertainty。
</PromptBlock>

呢個 sequence 嘅 hidden lesson：**Tax refund 嘅 expected value 通常細過你 panic-rushing 嘅 cost**。¥500 refund 唔值 missed flight。Trip prep 充分 = stress-free claim。

## 變化

### 變化 1：歐洲 VAT refund (Schengen + UK)

歐洲 VAT 同日本完全唔同 system：

<PromptBlock label="變化 1 — 歐洲 VAT refund (Schengen) 流程">
我去歐洲 [N 國 trip] [N 日]. Schengen + 可能 UK。請整 VAT refund framework：

🇪🇺 **歐洲 VAT (Value Added Tax) overview**

- Rate: 各國 different (Germany 19%, France 20%, Italy 22%, UK 20% etc)
- Tourists 持非 EU passport = qualified
- Refund typically 8-15% of purchase (after handling fee)

📋 **Process: ALL airport-level refund** (vs 日本 mostly store-level)

**Step 1 — At store**:
- 出示 passport (must be original, not copy)
- 入錢 ≥ EU country minimum threshold (typically €50-175 depending country)
- Store fills "Tax Free Form" (Global Blue / Planet / 部分 store proprietary)
- 你 拎走 form + receipt + 商品

**Step 2 — Departure airport (last EU country before leaving Schengen)**:
- Customs counter 印 stamp on tax free form
- May verify item (esp high-value)
- Items must be unused (esp luxury / electronics)
- 之後 mail form to operator (Global Blue / Planet)

**Step 3 — Refund processed**:
- Credit card refund: 2-6 週後 appear on card
- Cash refund: at airport refund counter (after customs stamp)
- Service fee: ~3-5% deducted

⚠️ **重要 distinction**

對於 multi-country trip:
- Customs stamp 喺 **last EU country before leaving Schengen / EU**
- e.g., 意大利 → 法國 → 西班牙 → 飛 HK: customs stamp 喺 西班牙 (last)
- e.g., 法國 → UK → 飛 HK: 喺法國 stamp before UK; UK 另計
- 唔可以 stamp 喺 intermediate stop

🏬 **常見 stores tax-free**:
- Hermès / LV / Chanel / 高 luxury brands
- 大型 department stores (Galeries Lafayette, KaDeWe)
- Some restaurants (高端) - 部分餐廳 / wine 可 tax-free
- Most chain stores opt-in

🚨 **Common reject reason**:
- Threshold not met (< €175 in France e.g.)
- Form filled wrong / signature missing
- Item shows usage (luxury bag 用過 visible wear)
- Receipt + form 不 match
- Passport 過期 / lost during trip

⏰ **Timing strategy at airport**

歐洲 customs queue 通常 比日本 longer:
- Peak times: 黃金週 / 暑假 / Christmas
- Allow 2-3 hour airport buffer
- Same-day refund cash counter close earlier than customs

💰 **Refund expectation**:
- Buy €1,000 in 法國 (VAT 20%): refund €166 typical (€1000 / 1.2 × 0.2 = €166.67, minus 3-5% fee = €130-150 net)
- Net refund: 13-15% of purchase

🏛 **Specific country quirks**:
- **France**: PABLO machine (faster than counter, available 24/7)
- **Germany**: customs stamp first, separate refund counter
- **Italy**: longest queue, prep buffer
- **UK** (post-Brexit): no longer Schengen, separate tax-free system, more restrictive

📦 **High-value purchase (>€1,000)**

Recommend:
- 用 credit card (insurance covers）
- Take photo at purchase
- Keep box + tag + dust bag
- 不 ship to HK (must take with you)
- Air-mail later costs more than tax-free saved

🛍 **Most-bought tax-free items by HK tourist**:
- Luxury handbags (LV / Chanel / Hermès)
- High-end watches (Rolex / Patek)
- Designer clothing (Burberry / Gucci)
- Wine + champagne (限 personal use carry-on)
- Perfume / cosmetics (注意 liquid carry-on limit)

語氣：detailed + caution about queue + handling fee。
</PromptBlock>
