## 一個 mindset

Personal budget 嘅 paradox: **越 detailed track, less likely sustain. 越 simple, more likely persist long-term.**

呢個 paradox 嘅 reason: budget app 100 category overhead high — most user abandon < 3 個月. Simple 1-page weekly review sustains 5+ 年.

呢個 mindset 嘅 reframe: Budget 唔係 「prove discipline」. Budget 係 **「automated system reduce decision fatigue」**. 你 don't 「budget every meal」 — 你 setup auto-saving, then spend remaining freely.

Claude 嘅 role 喺 budget: 你 alone analyze 3 個月 transaction 嘢 overwhelming. Claude container categorize + identify pattern + suggest specific leak. Insight emerged + action planned.

最後 reminder：

- ✅ **不 monthly budget perfect** = save HK$5-8k consistently. Compound 30 yr 顯著.
- ⚠️ **Subscription audit alone** = HK$200-500 immediate saving (most people). Low-hanging.
- 🎯 **Spending 不 「moral failure」**. 1 category 你 enjoy = OK. 6 leak categories 不 conscious = not OK.
