## 情境

而家 5 月底。

你細路 Marcus 8 歲，6 月開始放暑假。你 sign 咗佢 7 月中 4 日 3 夜 「海洋公園 Marine Camp 」(由小學 PE 老師組織)。

Day 1 form 出咗:
- Medical form (allergy / asthma / medication / 緊急 contact)
- Diet form (vegetarian / 過敏)
- **「家長介紹信 (1 頁 A4) — 等 group leader 認識你 細路, special 注意 if any」**

你 sit down 8pm 寫，13 分鐘後 stuck.

你 draft:

> 「Marcus is 8 years old. He is a nice boy. He likes swimming and Lego. He has no allergies.」

讀完自己 cringe。

你 try 加 detail:

> 「Marcus is somewhat shy with new groups. He may need encouragement to participate. He is a sensitive eater.」

讀完更 cringe。「Somewhat shy」 sound vague. 「Sensitive eater」 sounds like complaint. Organizer read 100 letters, 你 worry 你 letter 變 「demanding parent」 signal.

你 try 「professional」 tone:

> 「Marcus is a thoughtful and observant child who enjoys hands-on learning. He is most comfortable in structured environments with clear instructions.」

讀完 you realize — 呢段 描述 任何 個 細路 都 啱. 唔 specifically Marcus.

呢個 camp letter paradox：

- **「Honest detail」** 怕似 投訴
- **「Professional」怕似行貨
- **「Quirky 細節」** 怕被 organizer 視 為 「difficult parent」
- **太 short** organizer 唔 know how handle
- **太 long** organizer 唔 read

你 want: organizer **真 understand Marcus enough 不 force 佢 唔 啱 嘅 activity, 但 also 不 segregate / over-protect**.

呢篇用 Claude 30 分鐘 produce **honest but readable 1 頁 letter** — focus on 「decoded behavior」 + 「practical accommodation」 + 「what success looks like for THIS kid」.

**段 2: Personality decode (the unique part)**

「Marcus is initially shy in new groups (~30 minutes warm-up time). After warm-up, he engages well. He does NOT vocalize stress easily, but uses physical signal: clearing throat repeatedly, touching ear, becoming quiet, complaining of small ailment. Group leaders alert to these signal can offer Marcus a brief 1-on-1 check-in which usually resolves.」

「Marcus thrives in: structured activity with clear instruction, marine biology context, partner activity with 1 buddy.」

「Marcus struggles with: loud unpredictable sound, fast-changing instruction, large free-play (>15 kid).」

「Marcus is comfortable with: light teasing once warmed up, encouragement, learning new vocabulary, helping younger kids.」

**段 3: Communication (我 哋 want)**

- Daily 1 photo / brief message acceptable (no need detail)
- 任何 concern (medical / behavioral / homesick beyond normal) please call/WhatsApp immediately
- 我 唔 expect daily call from camp staff — quiet days = good signal
- If emergency: 我 (mom) cell, partner cell, 細妹's grandmother backup

**Closing**

- 1 sentence:「Marcus excited about this camp 因 marine animal — he's read [book name]. He may surprise 你 with detailed shark fact knowledge.」
- 1 sentence:「Thank you for taking time to read this. We trust 你 experience.」
- 簽 + date

📋 **Style**

- Total: 350-450 字 + minor format space
- 1 頁 A4 print OK
- Professional but warm
- Honest, 不 minimize 真 challenge
- 不 dramatize OR cliché
- Phone + email at top + bottom (for organizer convenience)

🚫 **Avoid**

- 「Marcus is a special child who needs careful handling」 (red flag for organizer)
- 「Please ensure Marcus...」 (demanding tone)
- Long medical history beyond practical
- Sister / family politics
- 「If anything happens please...」 (broad demand)
- Apologetic「I know this is many requirements」 (defensive)

✅ **Include**
- Practical「If X, then please Y」
- Decode behavioral signal
- Reassure 我 trust their experience
- 1 positive note about Marcus (animal interest)
- 我 contact info prominent

語氣：partner with organizer, not micromanaging.
</PromptBlock>

- 「You can call 我 anytime if you want.」 — 但 don't promise specific call schedule

**Day 5 前: pack together**
- Marcus 自己 揀 own clothes (limited choice: 你 lay out 2 options)
- 1 comfort item from home (familiar pillowcase / soft toy)

- Show packed bag layout

- 「You might feel a bit weird first night — that's normal. 我哋 used to feel that camp too.」

- 「Tomorrow morning 8am 我 drop you off. We will hug, then 我 walk away. You walk towards your group.」

- Over-promise:「You're going to LOVE it!」

- Pre-anxiety transfer: 你 own crying / 過度 worried face

- Casual tone, optimistic but realistic
- 答 questions honestly
- 1 secret family signal (specific hand wave, code word) for unspoken comfort
- 1 written note hidden in his bag「Have fun! Love, Mom」 (will find Day 2, mood boost)
- 公婆 / 親朋 visit before — distract excitement
- 不 marathon「last weekend before camp」 over-tight schedule

- Normal bedtime — 唔 special change
- 1 hug
- 「See you Day 5. 你 going to do great. Goodnight.」
- 你 自己 calm (Marcus mirror parent state)

🚗 **Drop-off day**
- Phone backup charged
- Tissue in pocket (for 你 own emotion)
- Quick hug, decisive walk away — don't linger
- Cry in car if needed (你 own emotion)

📞 **First 24 hr**
- 你 expect call: probably yes
- If call cry: short comfort + brief firm「You can do this. I will see 你 Day 5.」 don't extend call
- If no call: trust system. Organizer would have contacted if issue.

📅 **During camp**
- Daily 1 photo from organizer (you check phone, but 不 obsessive)
- 不 multiple call to organizer for update
- 不 unannounced 「我 around if 你 want」 visit

🏠 **Day 5 pickup**
- Be there 10 min early
- Marcus may be: tired / hyper / quiet / shy
- 1st 24 hr return — let him decompress
- Avoid bombarding「How was every detail!」
- Story comes out gradually over the week

🎯 **Post-camp**
- Praise specific:「I'm proud you tried sailing」
- 不要行貨「good job!」
- 1 small celebration (gelato, his choice)
- Marcus build confidence for next year overnight

我細路 [autism spectrum / ADHD / severe allergy / 哮喘 severe / 慢性 condition]. 同 standard letter 唔同.

📐 **Special needs adjustments**

📋 **Letter expand (1.5-2 頁 OK)**

**Diagnosis disclosure**
- Direct, 不 hide:「Marcus has autism, level 1 (formerly 'Asperger's')」
- 唔 sugar-coat:「he sometimes processes information differently」 awkward
- Diagnosis date + diagnosing professional

**Specific accommodation**
- Sensory sensitivity (loud noise / texture / smell)
- Pattern preference (routine / predictability)
- Social communication style (literal interpretation)
- Trigger + recovery: 「If Marcus melt down, give him 10 min quiet space, do NOT physical contact, then offer simple choice」

**Strengths**
- Hyper-focus area
- 特長 (memorize fact / pattern / 動物)
- Buddy match suggestion if camp can accommodate

**Group leader 訓練 prep**
- Recommend: 1 specific staff inform of diagnosis + accommodation
- Other staff: basic awareness, not over-disclose to peer
- Peer disclosure: 細路 own choice if at all

**Emergency**
- More 詳 escalation: 「If 你 unable handle, please WhatsApp 我 immediately — I can talk Marcus down phone」

**Reasonable expectation set**
- Marcus may not「fit in」 mainstream activity
- Acceptable for him sit out big group game
- Success measure for him: 1 friend made, learn 1 new fact
- NOT: become extrovert

🏥 **Medical sheet enhance**
- Allergy: full list + cross-contamination concern
- Medication: detailed dosing + timing
- Emergency: EpiPen location + when use
- Insurance + specialist

📞 **Pre-camp call to organizer**
- Recommended for high-need 細路
- 15-min phone call 1 個禮拜 前
- Discuss expectations both side
- Sometimes camp refer alternate camp if not equipped — better know early

🚨 **Be prepared:**
- Organizer may decline if 過 needs camp 能 provide. 唔 take personal.
- Specialized camp / smaller ratio may fit better

語氣：advocate strongly + realistic.
</PromptBlock>

## 一個 mindset

Camp letter 嘅 paradox: **你 want organizer 真 understand 你 細路, but 唔 want 細路 typecast as「needs accommodation」 細路.**

呢個 tension's resolution: **specific decoded behavior + reasonable accommodation > vague worry + open-ended request**.

呢個 mindset 嘅 reframe: 你 letter 真正 service 唔係 「protect 細路 from camp」 — 係 **bridge between 你嘅 deep knowledge of 細路 + organizer's general experience**. 兩者 combine = optimal experience.

Claude 嘅 role 喺 letter writing: 你 default mode 寫 letter 容易 either 過 detailed (anxiety dump) OR 過行貨（過度小心）. Claude help structure dumps 入 organizer-readable specific. Output: organizer 第一眼 「I can work with this kid」.

最後 reminder：

- ✅ **具體嘅「If X then Y」 instruction > 行貨 「he needs care」**. Organizer 揀 helpful detail.
- ⚠️ **Marcus 第 1 day cry phone call ≠ failure**. Day 1 transition real. Trust system.
- 🎯 **後 camp success metric ≠ best camper award**. = Marcus return home 一 thread of new confidence, 1 new fact about 動物, 1 sentence about new friend.

4 日後 Marcus 返到屋企. Some moments he 想 tell 你, some 唔 tell. Both OK.
