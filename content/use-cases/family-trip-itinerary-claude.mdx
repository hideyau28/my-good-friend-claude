## 情境

你 plan 緊 8 月家庭 trip 去大阪 5 日 4 夜。

家庭組成：

- 你（38, marketing manager）
- Partner（40, accountant）
- 8 歲女（升 P3，喜歡 Disney + Universal）
- 5 歲仔（K2，attention span 30 分鐘 max，meltdown trigger 包括「行得耐」/「餓」/「熱」）

- 食物日本 ramen / sushi / yakiniku
- Shop 心齋橋 + 道頓堀 + 美食巷
- 1 日 USJ
- 1 日 Kyoto day trip
- Nara 鹿如果可以加埋

- Avoid over-rush
- 細路 meltdown = 全屋企人 ruin day
- 預 daily 1-2 hr rest time
- Cash flow 唔好 over-budget

- Shopping for K-pop / toys

- 公園跑 + 玩水
- 食 udon / fries / 飯團

你 stare at list 半個鐘，realize problem：

- **Energy management**：5 歲仔 11am sharp 起 starvation tantrum；2pm 後 nap window；4pm 後 second wind 但易 meltdown

呢個 family trip planning 嘅 paradox：**4 個 traveller × 4 個 different need = 行程 90% 嘅 conflict tension**。

你 default 邊個 take care of?

- 8 歲女 + 大人 want = 5 歲仔 suffer
- 5 歲仔 anchor = 8 歲女 boring → 自身 meltdown
- 對半 split = 全 day 鬥嘴

呢篇用 Claude **dump 全家 preference + constraint，出 balanced 5 日 itinerary**——每日有大人 highlight + 細路 anchor + meal + 休息 buffer，minimize meltdown probability。

### 變化 1：Solo / couple trip（無細路）

冇 細路 constraint 嘅 trip 完全唔同：

<PromptBlock label="變化 1 — Solo / couple trip itinerary">
我哋 couple 去 [destination] [N 日]，冇 細路 / 老人家 constraint。

請整 solo / couple-optimized itinerary：

🎯 **No-kid trip 嘅 freedom**
- Earlier start time OK (沒 sleep schedule)
- Later evening OK (沒 bedtime)
- Skip family restaurant
- Take risk on niche local spot
- 戶外 long walk acceptable
- Late dinner OK

📐 **Day structure (vs family pattern)**
- AM: aggressive sightseeing start
- Lunch: 自由 — random street food / niche restaurant
- PM: continue or rest 由你 mood
- Evening: late dinner + bar / club / night market
- Night: late return acceptable

✅ **Activity types 適合 couple**
- Cooking class / cultural experience
- 行山 / outdoor adventure
- Museum + gallery (allow long viewing)
- Local market early morning
- Late-night izakaya 觀察 local life

💑 **Couple-specific consideration**
- 1 day each 「自己揀」(give partner full agency over 1 day)
- 1 「splurge」 meal experience (couple memory anchor)
- 1 「local immersion」 (homestay / class / volunteer)
- Avoid: rushing through every check-list item (rest = travel quality)

📊 **Budget allocation different**
- Higher % on experience (cooking class / private tour)
- Higher % on food (worth couple investment)
- Lower % on transit (less luggage to schlepp)

🚫 **Pitfall**
- Couple 但每日 100% together → over-saturation
- Schedule one half-day solo apiece is healthy

語氣：indulgent + autonomous。

### 變化 2：老人家 trip（行動較慢 + medical考慮）

帶父母 / 公公婆婆出 trip 完全唔同 dynamics：

<PromptBlock label="變化 2 — Senior parent trip planning">
我帶 [我父母 65 + 70 / 公公婆婆 75+] 出 trip [destination] [N 日]。佢哋[walking pace + medical condition + dietary]。

請整 senior parent-optimized itinerary：

🏥 **Senior-specific pre-trip prep**
- Medical clearance from family doctor
- Comprehensive travel insurance with senior coverage
- Medication 1.5x doses (買 extra)
- Doctor's letter for prescription (carry-on)
- 病歷 summary in English (for foreign hospital if emergency)
- 國際 SOS membership consideration

⏰ **Pace adjustment**
- Walking speed: 50-60% of younger person
- 1 main activity per day (max 2)
- Rest scheduling: every 90 minutes
- Avoid stairs heavy locations
- Lift / escalator preferred

🚶 **Mobility planning**
- Hotel: elevator + accessible bathroom
- Transport: avoid 8-hour bus, prefer train + taxi
- Activity proximity: cluster nearby to minimize transit
- Walking shoes critical (新鞋 唔好出 trip 試)
- 隨身 walker / cane (if applicable)

🍽 **Dietary consideration**
- Diabetes / 高血壓 specific menu
- Salt / oil reduction request when ordering
- Familiar 食物 anchor 1-2 meal daily (rice + simple cooked vege)
- Avoid raw seafood / 街邊 unverified
- Stomach upset response kit
- Tea preference (older parent 鐘意 hot tea on arrival)

🌡 **Climate consideration**
- Avoid extreme heat / cold destinations

## 一個 mindset

5 日家庭 trip 嘅 success metric 唔係 「我哋 hit 完所有 attraction」——係 **5 日後返 HK，全屋企人 fond memory + 想再 trip together**。

呢個 metric 嘅 implication：family trip planning 嘅 enemy 唔係 missed attraction，係 **cumulative meltdown that erodes family bond**。Day 3 全屋企人 hot + 累 + hungry 鬥嘴嘅 90 分鐘，比 missed Kyoto 嘅 disappointment 影響 deeper。

呢個 mindset 嘅 reframe：family itinerary 唔係 「optimize attraction count」——係 **engineer 4 個 different need 嘅 simultaneous satisfaction window**。每日有 1 個 大人 highlight + 1 個 細路 anchor + meal both like + 休息 = success。

Claude 嘅 role 喺 family trip：你 alone 估計 multi-person preference 嘅 capacity 有限。Claude help you systematically dump 全家 preference + constraint，再 output 一個 considered itinerary。**唔係 perfect itinerary — 係 thoughtful starting point that respects each family member**。

最後 reminder：

- ✅ **Itinerary 唔需要 100% follow**。Family trip nature flexible，預 30% buffer for 細路 mood / weather / 突發。
- ⚠️ **Daily 30-min adult moment 唔 optional**。Skip 一日你 OK，連續 5 日 skip 等於 burnout。
- 🎯 **Cumulative memory > daily efficiency**。8 歲女 + 5 歲仔 5 年後唔記得 itinerary detail，但記得「我哋一齊去 Osaka 嘅 8 月，5 歲仔 喺 Nara 餵鹿 笑到趴喺地」。Engineer for that moment。

Bon voyage, 屋企人。
