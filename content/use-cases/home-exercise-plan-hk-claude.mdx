### 2. 用呢個 prompt 出 8 週 plan

<PromptBlock label="完整 prompt — 8 week progressive home routine">
[paste 你 baseline dump]

請整 **8 週 progressive home exercise plan**，每週 5 日（休息 2 日），每日 15-25 分鐘：

📐 **Design principles** (你 必 follow):
- 低衝擊 (鄰居 friendly — no burpees, no jump squats. Modified bodyweight + dumbbell-based)
- Progressive (Week 1 simple → Week 8 challenging)
- Equipment minimum (mat + 1 對 dumbbell 3-5kg if added Week 3)
- 多元 (strength + mobility + light cardio - not single-modality plateau)
- Sustainable schedule (15-25 min cap)

🗓 **每週 structure**

**Mon - Strength upper + core (20 min)**
**Tue - Mobility + light cardio (15 min)**
**Wed - Strength lower + glute (20 min)**
**Thu - 休息 OR active recovery (walk only)**
**Fri - Full body circuit (25 min)**
**Sat - 長 mobility flow (15-20 min) + 戶外 walk separate 30 min**
**Sun - 休息**

📅 **Week 1-2: Foundation (no equipment)**

Goal: 建立 habit + identify 弱 area + form check

每日 15-min routine:
- 1 min mobility warm-up (neck, shoulder, hip circle)
- 12 min 主 workout
- 2 min cooldown (stretch)

主 workout examples:
- Mon: bodyweight squat × 10 × 3, wall push-up × 8 × 3, plank 20 sec × 3
- Wed: glute bridge × 12 × 3, side plank 15 sec each × 2, dead bug × 8 × 3
- 等等

📊 **Week 3-4: Add load + complexity**

Buy: 1 對 3kg dumbbell (HK$150-200 at Decathlon)

Add to routine:
- Goblet squat with dumbbell (load)
- Dumbbell row (back)
- Dumbbell shoulder press
- Increase rep range 12-15

時間：20 min

📈 **Week 5-6: Progressive overload**

If 3kg too easy: upgrade to 5kg (or add reps)

Add:
- Bulgarian split squat (single leg, balance)
- Push-up progression (from wall → incline → flat → modified)
- Plank progression (longer holds)
- Add 1 short cardio session (15 min jump rope replacement — high knee march if jumping noise)

時間：20 min

🎯 **Week 7-8: Consolidate + assess**

- 全部 movements proficient
- Final week: max-effort test (compare to Day 1)
- Plank time
- Push-up count
- Squat depth video record
- 樓梯 4 層 test
- Body composition photo (front / side / back)

📋 **Daily checklist (during plan):**
- [ ] Sleep 7+ hr previous night
- [ ] Hydrated (1 cup water before exercise)
- [ ] Form check (slower > more reps with bad form)
- [ ] 1-10 effort rating (target 6-8 for 主 session)
- [ ] Pain (sharp) vs discomfort (sustained burn) — sharp = STOP

🚨 **Red flag signal**
- Sharp pain joint / lower back > 1 day post-workout: STOP that exercise, consult physio
- Cardiovascular: chest pain / dizziness / disproportionate breathlessness during exercise → STOP + medical
- 持續 fatigue >1 週: under-recovery, reduce volume

🎒 **Equipment to buy (in order)**

Week 0:
- 1 mat (already have)

Week 3:
- 1 對 dumbbell 3kg or 5kg (HK$150-200 Decathlon)

Week 5 (optional):
- 1 mini resistance band (HK$60)
- 1 jump rope (HK$80) - or sub with high-knee march

DO NOT buy:
- Home cardio machine (HK 350 sqft 無位)
- Multiple dumbbell sizes (3kg or 5kg only)
- Smart-fit tracker (your phone time + manual log enough)

📝 **Daily log format (5 min after workout)**

```
WEEK X DAY Y
完成 routine: Y/N
Modifications: [if any]
Effort 1-10: 
Notable: [pain, energy, mood]
Next day adjust: 
```

🏋 **Mistake to avoid (HK home exercise specific)**
- 過早 jump training (鄰居 + injury)
- Treating Day 1 like personal best (build slow)
- Compare with social media 「6-week transformation」 (lighting + angle)
- Skip cooldown / stretch (mobility 同樣 重要)
- Quit Day 4 because 「沒 progress」 (visible change Week 3+)

- Week 4: clothes fit slightly differently
- Week 6: photo comparable change

⏰ **每日 schedule advice for HK busy schedule**
- AM (7am - 7:30am): 適合 strength session (cortisol natural elevation)
- PM (9pm - 9:30pm): 適合 mobility / light session (winds down for sleep)
- 唔 mid-day (lunch break 太短 + 唔 shower 接客)

語氣：specific + permission to modify + realistic timeline.

### 4. Adjust schedule for sick / travel / stress week

8 週 plan 唔 holiday-proof — 預備 disruption protocol：

<PromptBlock label="完整 prompt — Disruption adjustment protocol">
我 7-week mark (or whenever), encountering disruption:
- [Sick (cold / flu) 3-7 days]
- [Work crunch — 80 hr week]
- [Travel 4 days]
- [Family emergency requiring 重 ​mental load]

請 adjust：

🤒 **Sick disruption (cold / flu / 重 病)**
- Mild cold (no fever): light walk OK, skip strength
- Fever / severe: NO exercise, prioritize sleep + hydrate
- Recovery rule: 24 hr after fever break → light walk OK, 48 hr → resume strength
- Don't「make up」missed days. Continue from where you 左

💼 **Work crunch week**
- Reduce to 2 sessions/week (Tues + Sat)
- 10 min each (shorter, maintain habit)
- Skip cardio session, retain core movements
- Sleep 同 nutrition more critical than workout
- Resume normal schedule next week

✈️ **Travel disruption (3-7 days)**
- Pack 1 resistance band (lighter than dumbbell)
- 10-min hotel room routine: bodyweight only
- 1 daily 20-min walk outdoor (different environment 鞏 mood)
- Travel ≠ excuse skip entire week. Travel = lighter modality.

🧠 **Mental load week (家庭 crisis / break-up / job stress)**
- Don't quit, but reduce intensity
- Replace strength with mobility-only sessions (15-20 min)
- Skip workout sessions when emotionally exhausted
- Walks count (low cognitive load, mood-supporting)
- 同 mental health journal (separate prompt) 一齊 use

📅 **Re-entry after pause >1 week**
- Don't pick up where you 左 — drop 1-2 week back
- Re-introduce gradually:
  - Day 1: 10 min light session
  - Day 2: rest
  - Day 3: 15 min
  - Day 4: rest
  - Day 5: back to normal cadence
- Form check critical first 2 sessions back

🔄 **Restart protocol if multi-week pause**
- Restart from Week 1 of plan, but 加速:
  - Compress Week 1-2 → 5 days
  - Compress Week 3-4 → 7 days
  - Resume normal cadence Week 5

語氣：grace + non-judgmental. Disruption is normal, quit-from-disruption is the actual risk.
</PromptBlock>

呢個 step 通常 plan 之 後 才 諗 — 但係 actual 80% 嘅 quit reason。Pre-empt disruption response = 8-week plan 真 complete.

## 一個 mindset

Home exercise 嘅 paradox：你期 望 sustainable habit，但 progress visible 需要 weeks。

呢個 paradox 嘅 root: **default expectation 由 「7-day transformation」 social media 設定 - 但 actual physiology timeline 唔同。Body composition 8 週 才 visible change. Cardiovascular fitness 4 週 first measurable. Strength gain 6-8 週 noticeable.** 

呢個 mindset 嘅 reframe: **8 週 home plan 嘅 ROI 唔係 8 週後 你變另一個人**——係 8 週後 你 hold 一個 sustainable habit + 你 brain 嘅 「exercise habit pathway」 從 「frictional decision」 過渡到「automatic」。

Claude 嘅 role 喺 home exercise: 你 alone 設計 plan 嘅 problem 係 generic「3-week plan」 model 嘅 thinking — Claude 強迫 你 dump specifics 然後 build personalized progressive。Plan 一旦 set 你 brain freed up to just execute。

最後 reminder：

- ✅ **15-20 min daily 比 1 hr weekly 更 effective**。Habit formation + adaptation 都係 daily-frequency rewarded。
- ⚠️ **Sore ≠ injury 但 sharp ≠ DOMS**。Day 1 muscle soreness normal。Day 3+ 持續，sharp joint pain，唔 fix by rest 24 hr = physio / GP consultation。
- 🎯 **8 週後 你 真 progress = 你 仲 maintain habit, 唔係 metric improvement**。Metric improvement automatic happens。
