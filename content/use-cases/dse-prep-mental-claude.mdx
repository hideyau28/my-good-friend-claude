## 情境

3 月 8 日，11:34pm。

你 18 歲女兒 Cherrie 房門關 5 個鐘頭。你出去 lounge 倒水，聽到房入面 muffled 哭聲。

你 hesitate 3 秒，knock 門。

「⋯⋯ 唔好入嚟。」 嗰把聲已 broken.

「⋯⋯ 我攞杯水入嚟 OK?」

「⋯ 我嘥曬時間。我 全部 mock 都 fail. JUPAS 點交?」

你企喺門口，brain 飛快 try option:

- **Option A**: 「Cherrie 你 唔好咁諗，你 Form 5 嗰陣都係咁，最後 work out 嘅. Trust me.」 — 你 actually 唔 trust，你 just 唔知 say 咩.
- **Option B**: 「要唔要 揾 tutor 補多 1 科?」 — 你 internally know 已經 7 個 tutor, 加多 1 個唔 help.
- **Option C**: 「都 11:30pm la, sleep 啦」 — Cherrie 會 read 為「I dismiss 你 stress」.
- **Option D**: 「DSE 唔係 一切，仲有 IB / overseas」 — Cherrie 一直 hear 同 同學 父母 講同樣嘢，sense 為 cliché.
- **Option E**: 同 husband 講 「She's 過度 stressed, what do we do」 — 你哋兩個 mutual confused.

你 actual 講: 「我 outside if you need anything.」

你 return 客廳, 11:45pm, your 18 歲女兒 quiet, presumably 抹 tears 之後 try sleep, 第二朝 8am school bus.

呢個 cycle: Mock 後 emotional collapse → 你 well-meaning attempt → cherry 「mum 你唔 understand」 → 你 retreat → repeat.

你 misunderstanding 在: **你 brain default treat Cherrie's DSE prep as 「project to manage」**——你 think 你 role 係 optimize support efficiency. Tutor、補習班、 healthy food、quiet study room、daily check-in。

But Cherrie 嘅 actual need：唔係 多 1 個 tutor, 唔係 efficiency optimization. 佢 need: **1 個 emotionally regulated parent 唔 amplify 佢嘅 anxiety**.

呢篇用 Claude 幫家長 **reframe role + identify 邊個你 default reaction backfire + practice replacement**. 唔取代 family therapy — 但 daily 5 個 micro-interaction 改善 cumulative.

文末有 HK student mental health helpline. 急 case 唔好用 Claude 替代真人.

### 2. 用呢個 prompt 出 role reframe + interaction audit

<PromptBlock label="完整 prompt — Parent role reframe + interaction audit">

請幫我:

🎯 **Step 1: Role reframe**

我 default mode = 「performance coach + worry vehicle」
- 我 monitor exam result
- 我 monitor study hour
- 我 monitor mood
- 我 try optimize everything
- 副作用: cherrie sense 我 anxiety = amplification

請 propose alternative role frame: 「emotional regulator + 後勤 support 唔 monitor performance」
- 我 take care of: physical environment, food, sleep enable
- 我 取 唔 take care of: study content, exam result, motivation
- 我 explicit: 「Your DSE result 唔 reflect on me as parent or you as person」

⚠️ **Step 2: Audit 我 default reaction**

對於 typical scenario, identify 我 default backfire:

**Scenario A: Cherrie 房 11pm 仍 light on**
- Default backfire: knock door「Hey 唔好太晚 lah」 OR silently anxious
- Why backfire: monitor signal = 你 anxiety 「我注視你」
- Better: 唔 knock, 唔 monitor. 唔 know is OK. If concerned: leave 1 cup water + small note「I made tea, if you want — outside your door」then leave.

**Scenario B: Mock result 出, 21 分**
- Default backfire: 「How can we improve next mock?」OR「Did you see Mr Chan's mock plan?」
- Why backfire: response treats result as failure to optimize
- Better: 「Result for review with school counselor, not me. 你 want talk 我傾, 想 alone 都 OK.」

**Scenario C: Cherrie cries at dinner table**
- Default backfire: 「What's wrong? Tell me. We can solve this together.」 OR 「DSE 唔 important than 健康!」
- Why backfire: rushing to fix emotional state interrupts feeling processing
- Better: silent presence, hand on shoulder, 「I'm here. No rush to talk.」

**Scenario D: Cherrie 1 個 月 唔 see friend**
- Default backfire: 「You should see [friend name], 你哋好耐 唔 hang out」
- Why backfire: adding social obligation to overwhelm
- Better: 唔 push. If 你 obviously have friend gathering planned, 「You're invited if you want, completely OK to skip.」

**Scenario E: 公婆 phone「Cherrie 加油」**
- Default backfire: silent acceptance OR overcorrect to cherrie 「don't listen to grandma」
- Why backfire: 你 ambiguity = cherrie internalize
- Better: 你 phone 公婆 separately 1 次, 「我 know you 關心, but 大量 「加油」 message 對 cherrie 而家 反而 harmful. 請 just 「I love you」 / 「You will be OK」 messaging. 唔 mention result / 試題.」

請 identify 5-10 我 default scenario + replacement script, 我 print 出嚟 內化.

語氣：specific replacement, not just abstract advice.
</PromptBlock>

Claude 出 personalized audit。Print 出嚟。**Day 1-7 strict practice 替換** — 你會 feel awkward (你 default mode 強). Day 8-14 開始 second nature.

### 3. Daily 5-min check-in script (替代 monitoring)

呢個 step 唔 add new burden — 替代 existing monitoring:

<PromptBlock label="完整 prompt — Daily 5-min low-pressure check-in script">
我每日 want check-in cherrie. 想 replace「Performance monitoring」 with「emotional presence」.

請整 daily 5-min ritual script:

⏰ **Timing**
- 唔係 cherrie return 家 立即 (decompression need)
- 唔係 cherrie study session 中 (interruption)
- 唔係 11pm (sleep zone)
- 適合: dinner mid-meal (natural conversation) OR weekend morning slow

🗣 **3 sentence template (you say, optional response)**

**Sentence 1: Physical check**
- 「How's 你 body feeling today?」(specific, factual)
- 唔 「How are you」 (vague + Cherrie reflex「fine」)
- Cherrie answer: 「Tired」「OK」「back hurts」「good」
- Your response: brief acknowledge, 「OK. 想 我 [make 你 tea / 准備 bath / 揾你 painkiller]?」

**Sentence 2: 1 small thing reveal**
- 你 share 1 specific moment from 你 day, neutral
- 「I had a meeting that ran late and almost missed dinner.」
- 「Your sister told me a funny story from school.」
- 「I read a book recommendation, want me to share?」
- Purpose: cherrie sees you as person, not anxious監視者

**Sentence 3: Open door (NOT obligation)**
- 「If you 想 talk about anything — completely up to you — 我 around tonight after 9pm.」
- 唔 「DSE 點」/「test 點」
- 唔 require response
- Cherrie 可以 nod, mumble, ignore — 不 take personally

**That's it.** 唔 follow-up。如 cherrie volunteer 大 conversation: present without fixing. 如 cherrie 簡 nod and leave: success — 唔 push.

🚫 **Avoid these phrases (默 internal kill list)**
- 「How was your day?」 (overload)
- 「Did you study?」 (monitor)
- 「Did you eat?」 (nag)
- 「Your friend X just got into Y university」 (comparison)
- 「I'm proud of you」 (only if specifically warranted)
- 「You'll be fine」 (dismissive of stress)
- 「Just do your best」 (cliché)

✅ **OK to say**
- 「I'm here.」
- 「No need to talk if not ready.」
- 「Tea?」
- 「Want me to leave you alone tonight?」
- 「I trust you to manage your time.」 (if 你 actually do)
- 「Sleep when 你 want.」 (DSE prep marathon, late sleep is real)
- 「What 你 want me to NOT do?」 (ask cherrie)
- 「If 你 want, I can do [specific thing]. Or not.」

📋 **Weekly checkpoint (NOT daily)**

每週末 5 分鐘 with cherrie:
- 「想 我 next week handle 邊 樣 differently?」
- 「Anything I'm doing 你 want me to STOP?」
- Reverse the power: 你 take cherrie 意見 seriously
- 唔 defend default mode

🚨 **Red flag escalation**
持續 >2 週 嘅:
- Sleep 少於 4 hr / night
- 食 minimal / refuses eat with family
- 不能 study (sit but cannot read)
- 朋友 contact dropped 100%
- Self-harm mention (任何 form)
- 「I want this to be over」 (ambiguous statement)

= 即 escalate 到 school counsellor / GP / mental health professional. 唔 wait「DSE 完 再 worry」.

語氣：respectful of student autonomy + 唔 patronize.
</PromptBlock>

呢個 script 第一週 difficult — 你 default mode 強. Cherrie 第 3-5 日 may notice difference, possibly 試 你. Day 10+ 你 noticeable 自己 less anxious because 不再 over-functioning.

### 變化 1：細路 mental crisis (acute)

如果 cherrie 出現 self-harm thought / 拒絕 起床 / function:

<PromptBlock label="變化 1 — Acute mental crisis 處理">
細路 acute crisis: [self-harm thought / can't get out of bed / 持續 cry / express not wanting to live].

🚨 **IMMEDIATE (today)**

1. **本人 唔 alone**
   - 你 / partner 1 person stay with cherrie (uninterrupted)
   - 24 hr buddy system
   - 唔 leave alone in room with locked door

2. **Verbal de-escalate**
   - 「I am here. We will get through this together.」
   - 「Your feelings 真實. I 唔 dismiss.」
   - 唔 problem-solve immediately
   - 唔 promise outcomes 你 can't deliver

3. **遠離 means**
   - 鎖 / remove medication, sharp object, alcohol
   - 不 confrontational, 不 declare action — just do

📞 **Within 24 hr**

1. **HK 24-hr helpline**
   - The Samaritans HK: 2389 2222 (Eng) / 2896 0000 (multi-language)
   - Suicide Prevention Services: 2389 2227
   - 撒瑪利亞防止自殺會: 2389 2222

2. **GP / school counselor**
   - Same-day GP appointment (mental health referral)
   - School counsellor inform 立即
   - 唔 worry 「stigma」 — confidentiality 醫 staff 守

3. **如 cherrie refuses help**
   - Cannot force adult 18+
   - 但 你 always express care + availability
   - 直接 emergency room if active self-harm threat

🏥 **Mental health professional pathway**

- GP refer → psychiatrist (for assessment) OR clinical psychologist (for therapy)
- HK public: 6-12 month wait at HA psychiatric clinic
- HK private: 養和 / 中環 private clinics 2-4 週 typically
- Insurance check: most policy 包 mental health since 2021 amendment
- Cost: psychiatrist HK$1500-3500 / session private

📋 **DSE-specific**

- DSE special consideration (extension, separate room, etc.) — apply through school counsellor + doctor's note
- DSE deferred to next year — feasible if doctor recommend
- Drop DSE 完全 — non-irreversible decision, 6-12 個月 後 retake or alternative path
- 唔 frame as「lost a year」 — 「prioritized health, restart on solid ground」

🚫 **Don'ts during acute crisis**
- 「You're letting us down」 (NEVER)
- 「Other people 都 stressed, 你 spoiled」 (NEVER)
- 「I'm worried」 (express care, but 唔 overload guilt)
- Force eating / studying
- Family meeting with 公婆 / extended family during acute phase

🆘 **Active suicidal ideation = ER**

如 cherrie state explicit suicidal plan / has taken means / 顯著 immediate danger:
- Direct A&E (急症室)
- AED 24-hr admit + assessment
- 唔 hesitate 「too dramatic」
- Better err 過於 cautious

語氣：calm + decisive + 不 minimize 嘅 specific action.
</PromptBlock>

## 一個 mindset

DSE prep year 嘅 parent paradox: **你 想 help 嘅程度 越大，越 risky 你 over-function 嚟 cherrie 嘅 disservice**.

呢個 paradox 嘅 mechanism: 你 well-intentioned action (extra tutor, daily check-in, study schedule design) 透視 cherrie internal 為 signal「Mom doesn't trust me 」or「Mom worried 我 fail」 — both interpretation increase her cortisol = worsen prep.

呢個 mindset 嘅 reframe: 你 真正 「support」 = active de-escalate own anxiety + 提供 stable environment. 唔 study 内容 micromanage. 唔 outcome attachment.

Claude 嘅 role 喺 parent during DSE year: 你 alone navigate complex emotional dynamics 容易 reactive. Claude container 提供 distance — 你 dump scenario, see alternative script before reactive. Daily 5 個 alternative response practiced over 6 個月 = cumulative parent能力 升級.

最後 reminder：

- ✅ **你 唔 「fix」 cherrie 嘅 DSE prep. 你 「co-exist」 with her stress while 唔 amplify.**
- ⚠️ **Crisis warning sign continue >2 week = 唔 wait DSE 完**. 心理 health 真 emergency.
- 🆘 **DSE result 唔 reflect parent worth**. 你 self-worth 由 你 own life shape, 唔 cherrie outcome.
